2.2.6.1 Anulomapuggala
Pavattivāra
Nirodhavāra
Atītānāgatavāra
Anulomapuggala
118.
520»  Yassa kāyasaṅkhāro nirujjhittha tassa vacīsaṅkhāro nirujjhissatīti?
521Savitakkasavicārapacchimacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe avitakkaavicārapacchimacittasamaṅgīnaṃ yassa cittassa anantarā avitakkaavicāraṃ pacchimacittaṃ uppajjissati tesaṃ kāyasaṅkhāro nirujjhittha, no ca tesaṃ vacīsaṅkhāro nirujjhissati. Itaresaṃ tesaṃ kāyasaṅkhāro ca nirujjhittha vacīsaṅkhāro ca nirujjhissati.
522«  Yassa vā pana…pe… ? Āmantā.
523»  Yassa kāyasaṅkhāro nirujjhittha tassa cittasaṅkhāro nirujjhissatīti?
524Pacchimacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ kāyasaṅkhāro nirujjhittha, no ca tesaṃ cittasaṅkhāro nirujjhissati. Itaresaṃ tesaṃ kāyasaṅkhāro ca nirujjhittha cittasaṅkhāro ca nirujjhissati.
525«  Yassa vā pana…pe… ? Āmantā.
