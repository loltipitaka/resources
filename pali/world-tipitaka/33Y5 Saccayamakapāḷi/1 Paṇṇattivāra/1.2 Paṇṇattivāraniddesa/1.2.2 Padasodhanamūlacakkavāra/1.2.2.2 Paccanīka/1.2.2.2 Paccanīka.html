16.
158»  Na dukkhaṃ na dukkhasaccanti?
159Kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ cetasikaṃ dukkhaṃ ṭhapetvā avasesaṃ na dukkhaṃ, dukkhasaccaṃ. Dukkhañca dukkhasaccañca ṭhapetvā avasesaṃ na ceva dukkhaṃ na ca dukkhasaccaṃ.
