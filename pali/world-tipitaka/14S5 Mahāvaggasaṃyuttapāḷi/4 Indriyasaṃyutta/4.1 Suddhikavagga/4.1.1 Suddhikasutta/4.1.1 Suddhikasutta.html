4.1.1 Suddhikasutta
Indriyasaṃyutta
Suddhikavagga
Suddhikasutta
471.
1000Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Tatra kho bhagavā etadavoca. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.
1001Paṭhamaṃ.
