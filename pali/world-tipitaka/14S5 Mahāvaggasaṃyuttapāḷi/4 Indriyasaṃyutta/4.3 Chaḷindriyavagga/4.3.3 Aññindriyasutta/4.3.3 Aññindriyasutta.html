4.3.3 Aññindriyasutta
Indriyasaṃyutta
Chaḷindriyavagga
Aññindriyasutta
493.
1065“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ, aññindriyaṃ, aññātāvindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī”ti.
1066Tatiyaṃ.
