12.2.7 Avijjāsutta
Saccasaṃyutta
Dhammacakkappavattanavagga
Avijjāsutta
1087.
2249Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “‘avijjā, avijjā’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamā nu kho, bhante, avijjā; kittāvatā ca avijjāgato hotī”ti? “Yaṃ kho, bhikkhu, dukkhe aññāṇaṃ, dukkhasamudaye aññāṇaṃ, dukkhanirodhe aññāṇaṃ, dukkhanirodhagāminiyā paṭipadāya aññāṇaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhu, avijjā; ettāvatā ca avijjāgato hotīti.
2250Tasmātiha, bhikkhu, ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo…pe…  ‘ayaṃ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.
2251Sattamaṃ.
