8.2.3 Dibbasotasutta
Anuruddhasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Dibbasotasutta
911.
1597“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā dibbāya sotadhātuyā visuddhāya atikkantamānusikāya ubho sadde suṇāmi dibbe ca mānuse ca ye dūre santike cā”ti.
1598Tatiyaṃ.
