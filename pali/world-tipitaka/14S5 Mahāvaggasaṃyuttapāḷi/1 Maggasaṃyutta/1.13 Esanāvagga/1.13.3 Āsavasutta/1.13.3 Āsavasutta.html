1.13.3 Āsavasutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Esanāvagga
Āsavasutta
163.
314“Tayome, bhikkhave, āsavā. Katame tayo? Kāmāsavo, bhavāsavo, avijjāsavo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āsavā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṃ āsavānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe…  ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
315Tatiyaṃ.
