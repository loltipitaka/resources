1.3.1 Micchattasutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Micchattavagga
Micchattasutta
21.
88Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Micchattañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammattañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, micchattaṃ? Seyyathidaṃ—  micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchattaṃ. Katamañca, bhikkhave, sammattaṃ? Seyyathidaṃ—  sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammattan”ti.
89Paṭhamaṃ.
