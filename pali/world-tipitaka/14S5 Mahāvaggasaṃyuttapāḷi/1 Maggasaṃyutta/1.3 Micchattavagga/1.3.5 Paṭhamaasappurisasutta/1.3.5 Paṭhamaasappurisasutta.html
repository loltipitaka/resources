1.3.5 Paṭhamaasappurisasutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Micchattavagga
Paṭhamaasappurisasutta
25.
98Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Asappurisañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sappurisañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asappuriso? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco micchādiṭṭhiko hoti, micchāsaṅkappo, micchāvāco, micchākammanto, micchāājīvo, micchāvāyāmo, micchāsati, micchāsamādhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asappuriso.
99Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sappuriso? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco sammādiṭṭhiko hoti, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvāco, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sappuriso”ti.
100Pañcamaṃ.
