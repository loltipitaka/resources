1.4.8 Dutiyabrahmaññasutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Paṭipattivagga
Dutiyabrahmaññasutta
38.
137Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Brahmaññañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmaññatthañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmaññaṃ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ—  sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmaññaṃ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, brahmaññattho? Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmaññattho”ti.
138Aṭṭhamaṃ.
