1.4.10 Dutiyabrahmacariyasutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Paṭipattivagga
Dutiyabrahmacariyasutta
40.
141Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Brahmacariyañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmacariyatthañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ—  sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyattho? Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyattho”ti.
142Dasamaṃ.
143Paṭipattivaggo catuttho.
144Tassuddānaṃ
145
Paṭipatti paṭipanno ca,
viraddhañca pāraṅgamā;
Sāmaññena ca dve vuttā,
brahmaññā apare duve;
Brahmacariyena dve vuttā,
vaggo tena pavuccatīti.
