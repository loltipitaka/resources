1.1.1.5.1 Paccanīkānuloma

Puggalakathā
Okāsasaccikaṭṭha
Paccanīkānuloma
14.
52× Puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Āmantā. Sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe.
53Ājānāhi niggahaṃ. Hañci puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena, tena vata re vattabbe—  “sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā.
54No ce pana vattabbe—  “sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā…pe… .
55Chaṭṭho niggaho.
