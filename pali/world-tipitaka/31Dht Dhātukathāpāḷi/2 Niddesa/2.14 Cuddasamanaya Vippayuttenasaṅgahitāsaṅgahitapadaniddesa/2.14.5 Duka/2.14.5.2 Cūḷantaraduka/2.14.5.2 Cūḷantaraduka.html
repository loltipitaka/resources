2.14.5.2 Cūḷantaraduka
Niddesa
Cuddasamanaya Vippayuttenasaṅgahitāsaṅgahitapadaniddesa
Duka
Cūḷantaraduka
496.
617Appaccayehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  asaṅkhatehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  sanidassanehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  sappaṭighehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  rūpīhi dhammehi ye dhammā…  lokuttarehi dhammehi ye dhammā vippayuttā…  te dhammā catūhi khandhehi dvīhāyatanehi aṭṭhahi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi dasahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
497.
618Lokiyehi dhammehi ye dhammā vippayuttā…  te dhammā catūhi khandhehi dvīhāyatanehi dvīhi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi soḷasahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
