2.1.7.5 Saṃkiliṭṭhattika
Niddesa
Paṭhamanaya saṅgahāsaṅgahapadaniddesa
Tika
Saṃkiliṭṭhattika
87.
133Asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikā dhammā pañcahi khandhehi dvādasahāyatanehi aṭṭhārasahi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Na kehici khandhehi na kehici āyatanehi na kāhici dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
