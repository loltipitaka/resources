264Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava, naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
265Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
266Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
