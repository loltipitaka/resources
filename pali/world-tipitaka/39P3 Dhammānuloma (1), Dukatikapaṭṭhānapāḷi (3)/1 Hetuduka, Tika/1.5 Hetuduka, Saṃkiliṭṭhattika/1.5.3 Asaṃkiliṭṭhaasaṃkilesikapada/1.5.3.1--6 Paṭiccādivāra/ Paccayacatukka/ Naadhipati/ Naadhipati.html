204.
380Hetuṃ asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. Hetuṃ asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (2)
381Nahetuṃ asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. Nahetuṃ asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (2) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
205.
382Naadhipatiyā cha, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
383Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā cha. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
384Naadhipatipaccayā hetuyā cha. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
