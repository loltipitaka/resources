Adukkhamasukhāyavedanāyasampayuttapada
Pañhāvāra
Paccanīyuddhāra
106.
180Hetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo hetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
107.
181Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, paccanīyaṃ.)
182Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ, anulomapaccanīyaṃ.)
183Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, paccanīyānulomaṃ.)
184(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
185Hetudukavedanāttikaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
