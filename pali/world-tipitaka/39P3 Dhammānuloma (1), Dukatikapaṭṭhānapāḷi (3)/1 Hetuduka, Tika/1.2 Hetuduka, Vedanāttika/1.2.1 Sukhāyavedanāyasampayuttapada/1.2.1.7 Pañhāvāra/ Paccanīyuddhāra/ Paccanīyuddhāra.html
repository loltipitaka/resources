81.
135Hetu sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo hetussa sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
82.
136Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava…pe…  noavigate nava. (Paccanīyaṃ.)
137Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ, anulomapaccanīyaṃ.)
138Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, paccanīyānulomaṃ.)
139(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
