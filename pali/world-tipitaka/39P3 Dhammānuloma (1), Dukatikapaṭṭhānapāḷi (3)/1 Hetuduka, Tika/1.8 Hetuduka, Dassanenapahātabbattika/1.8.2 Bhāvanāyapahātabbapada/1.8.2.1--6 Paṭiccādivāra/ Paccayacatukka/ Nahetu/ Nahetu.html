541Nahetuṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
269.
542Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
543Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
544Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
