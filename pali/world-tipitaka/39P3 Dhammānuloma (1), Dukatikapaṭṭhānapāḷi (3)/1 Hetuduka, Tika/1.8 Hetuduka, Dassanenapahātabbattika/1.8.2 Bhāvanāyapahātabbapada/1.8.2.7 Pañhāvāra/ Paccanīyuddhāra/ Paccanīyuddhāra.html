272.
549Hetu bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo hetussa bhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
273.
550Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
551Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
552Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
553(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
