Nahetu-naadhipati
527.
1185Nahetuṃ ajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ ajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (2)
1186Hetuṃ ajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
528.
1187Nahetuyā dve, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1188Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1189Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
