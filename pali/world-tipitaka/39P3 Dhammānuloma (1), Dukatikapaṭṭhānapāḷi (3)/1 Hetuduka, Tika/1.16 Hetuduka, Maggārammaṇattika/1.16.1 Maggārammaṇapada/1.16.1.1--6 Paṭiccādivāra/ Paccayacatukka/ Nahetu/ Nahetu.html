455.
995Nahetuṃ maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu maggārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
456.
996Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
997Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
998Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
