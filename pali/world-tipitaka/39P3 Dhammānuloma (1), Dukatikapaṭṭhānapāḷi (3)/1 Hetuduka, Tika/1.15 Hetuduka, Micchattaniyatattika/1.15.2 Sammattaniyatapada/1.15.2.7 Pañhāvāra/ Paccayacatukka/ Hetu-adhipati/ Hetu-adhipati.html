Hetu-adhipati
441.
958Hetu sammattaniyato dhammo hetussa sammattaniyatassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
959Hetu sammattaniyato dhammo hetussa sammattaniyatassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātādhipati…  tīṇi.
960Nahetu sammattaniyato dhammo nahetussa sammattaniyatassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātādhipati…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
442.
961Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā cha, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, kamme tīṇi, āhāre tīṇi, indriye nava, jhāne tīṇi, magge nava, sampayutte nava, atthiyā nava, avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
