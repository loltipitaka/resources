212.
399Nahetuṃ savitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu savitakkasavicāro dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā…  dve.
400Hetuṃ savitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu savitakkasavicāro dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
213.
401Nahetuyā dve, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
402Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
403Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
