Hetu
1.
2Hetuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu kusalo ca nahetu kusalo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
2.
3Nahetuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu kusalo ca nahetu kusalo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
3.
4Hetuṃ kusalañca nahetuṃ kusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuṃ kusalañca nahetuṃ kusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuṃ kusalañca nahetuṃ kusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu kusalo ca nahetu kusalo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
