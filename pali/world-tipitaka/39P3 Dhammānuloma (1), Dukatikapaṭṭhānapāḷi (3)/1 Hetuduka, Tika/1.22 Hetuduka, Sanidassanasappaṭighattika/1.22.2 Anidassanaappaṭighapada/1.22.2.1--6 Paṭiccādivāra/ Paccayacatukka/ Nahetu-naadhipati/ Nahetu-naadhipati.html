547.
1224Nahetuṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (2)
1225Hetuṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā…  tīṇi.
1226Hetuṃ anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
548.
1227Nahetuyā dve, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava…pe…  napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte nava, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1228Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1229Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
