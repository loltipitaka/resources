603Hetu bhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo hetussa bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

604Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
605Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
606Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
