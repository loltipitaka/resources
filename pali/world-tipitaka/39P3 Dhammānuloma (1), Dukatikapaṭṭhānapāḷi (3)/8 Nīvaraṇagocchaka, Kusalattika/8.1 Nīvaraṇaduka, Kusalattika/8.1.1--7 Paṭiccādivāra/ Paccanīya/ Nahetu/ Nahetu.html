5.
1901Nīvaraṇaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nīvaraṇo akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchānīvaraṇaṃ uddhaccanīvaraṇaṃ paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṃ. (1)
1902Nonīvaraṇaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nīvaraṇo akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṃ. (1)
1903Nīvaraṇañca nonīvaraṇañca akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nīvaraṇo akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchānīvaraṇañca uddhaccanīvaraṇañca sampayuttake ca khandhe paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṃ. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
