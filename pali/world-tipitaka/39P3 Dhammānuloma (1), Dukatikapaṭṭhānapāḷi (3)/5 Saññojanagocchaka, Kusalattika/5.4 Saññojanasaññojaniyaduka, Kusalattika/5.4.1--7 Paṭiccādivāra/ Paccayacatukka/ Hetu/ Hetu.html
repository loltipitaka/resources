Hetu
28.
1756Saññojaniyañceva no ca saññojanaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojaniyo ceva no ca saññojano kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
29.
1757Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1758(Sahajātavārepi…pe…  pañhāvārepi sabbattha ekaṃ.)
30.
1759Saññojanañceva saññojaniyañca akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojano ceva saññojaniyo ca akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
31.
1760Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1761Nahetuyā tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1762(Sahajātavāropi…pe…  sampayuttavāropi paṭiccavārasadisā.)
32.
1763Saññojano ceva saññojaniyo ca akusalo dhammo saññojanassa ceva saññojaniyassa ca akusalassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
33.
1764Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  upanissaye āsevane nava, kamme āhāre indriye jhāne tīṇi, magge sampayutte nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
34.
1765Nahetuyā nava naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1766Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1767Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1768(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
35.
1769Saññojaniyañceva no ca saññojanaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojaniyo ceva no ca saññojano abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
36.
1770Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
