2001Parāmaṭṭhañceva no ca parāmāsaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca parāmaṭṭho ceva no ca parāmāso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
2002Parāmāsañceva parāmaṭṭhaṃ akusalañca parāmaṭṭhañceva no ca parāmāsaṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca parāmaṭṭho ceva no ca parāmāso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
26.
2003Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe pañca…pe…  avigate pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
2004(Sahajātavārampi…pe…  sampayuttavārampi vitthāretabbaṃ.)
27.
2005Parāmaṭṭho ceva no ca parāmāso akusalo dhammo parāmaṭṭhassa ceva no ca parāmāsassa akusalassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
28.
2006Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  avigate pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
2007(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

2008Parāmaṭṭhañceva no ca parāmāsaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca parāmaṭṭho ceva no ca parāmāso abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
