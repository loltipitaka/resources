4.3.5 Dhammasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Dhammasutta
182.
1333“Dhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi adhammañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, adhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, adhammo.
1334Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, dhammo”ti.
1335Pañcamaṃ.
