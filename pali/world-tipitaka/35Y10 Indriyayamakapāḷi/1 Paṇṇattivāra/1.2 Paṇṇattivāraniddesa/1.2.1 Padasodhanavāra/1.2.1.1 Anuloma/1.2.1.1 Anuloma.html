Anuloma
94.
558»  Cakkhu cakkhundriyanti?
559Dibbacakkhu paññācakkhu cakkhu, na cakkhundriyaṃ. Cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhu ceva cakkhundriyañca.
560«  Cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhūti? Āmantā.
561»  Sotaṃ sotindriyanti?
562Dibbasotaṃ taṇhāsotaṃ sotaṃ, na sotindriyaṃ. Sotindriyaṃ sotañceva sotindriyañca.
563«  Sotindriyaṃ sotanti? Āmantā.

568»  Kāyo kāyindriyanti?
569Kāyindriyaṃ ṭhapetvā avaseso kāyo, na kāyindriyaṃ. Kāyindriyaṃ kāyo ceva kāyindriyañca.
