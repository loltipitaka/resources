2.1.10 Anupubbanirodhasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Khemavagga
Anupubbanirodhasutta
61.
426“‘Anupubbanirodho, anupubbanirodho’ti, āvuso, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, āvuso, anupubbanirodho vutto bhagavatā”ti?
427“Idhāvuso, bhikkhu vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati. Ettāvatāpi kho, āvuso, anupubbanirodho vutto bhagavatā pariyāyena…pe… .
428Puna caparaṃ, āvuso, bhikkhu sabbaso nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṃ samatikkamma saññāvedayitanirodhaṃ upasampajja viharati, paññāya cassa disvā āsavā parikkhīṇā honti. Ettāvatāpi kho, āvuso, anupubbanirodho vutto bhagavatā nippariyāyenā”ti.
429Dasamaṃ.
