2.5.1
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Rāgapeyyāla
93.
488“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya nava dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame nava? Asubhasaññā, maraṇasaññā, āhāre paṭikūlasaññā, sabbaloke anabhiratasaññā, aniccasaññā, anicce dukkhasaññā, dukkhe anattasaññā, pahānasaññā, virāgasaññā—  rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime nava dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
