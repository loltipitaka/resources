129Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ vipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho navipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
130Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ vipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho navipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.
