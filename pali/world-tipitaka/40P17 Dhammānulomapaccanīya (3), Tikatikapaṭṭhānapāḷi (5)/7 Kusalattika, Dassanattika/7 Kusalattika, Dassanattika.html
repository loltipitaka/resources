47Akusalaṃ dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
48Akusalaṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
49Abyākataṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naabyākato nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
