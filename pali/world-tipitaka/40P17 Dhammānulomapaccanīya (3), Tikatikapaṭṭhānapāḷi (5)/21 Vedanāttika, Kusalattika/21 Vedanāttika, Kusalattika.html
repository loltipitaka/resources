38.
103Sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto abyākato dhammo nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa naabyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
104Ārammaṇe ekavīsati, adhipatiyā dasa, anantare cha…pe…  upanissaye ekavīsati, natthiyā vigate cha. (Dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaabyākatamūlaṃ adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaabyākatamūlampi kātabbaṃ.)
