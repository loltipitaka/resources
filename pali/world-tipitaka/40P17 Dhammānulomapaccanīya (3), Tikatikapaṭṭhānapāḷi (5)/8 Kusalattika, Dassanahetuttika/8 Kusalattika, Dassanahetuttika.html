Kusalattika, Dassanahetuttika
20.
50Akusalaṃ dassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
51Akusalaṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
52Akusalaṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
53Abyākataṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naabyākato nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
