10 Kusalattika, Sekkhattika
Kusalattika, Sekkhattika
22.
57Kusalaṃ sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Dve mūlāni.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
58Abyākataṃ asekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
59Abyākataṃ nevasekkhanāsekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naabyākato nanevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca…pe…  āsevane tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.
