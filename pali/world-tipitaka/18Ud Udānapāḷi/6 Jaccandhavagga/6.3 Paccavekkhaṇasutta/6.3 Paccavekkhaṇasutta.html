6.3 Paccavekkhaṇasutta
Jaccandhavagga
Paccavekkhaṇasutta
53.
486Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā attano aneke pāpake akusale dhamme pahīne paccavekkhamāno nisinno hoti, aneke ca kusale dhamme bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gate.
487Atha kho bhagavā attano aneke pāpake akusale dhamme pahīne viditvā aneke ca kusale dhamme bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gate tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi— 
488
“Ahu pubbe tadā nāhu,
nāhu pubbe tadā ahu;
Na cāhu na ca bhavissati,
na cetarahi vijjatī”ti. (64)
489Tatiyaṃ.
