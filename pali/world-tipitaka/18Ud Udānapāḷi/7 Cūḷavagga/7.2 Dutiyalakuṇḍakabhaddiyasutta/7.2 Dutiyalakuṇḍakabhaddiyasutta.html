7.2 Dutiyalakuṇḍakabhaddiyasutta
Cūḷavagga
Dutiyalakuṇḍakabhaddiyasutta
62.
608Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā sāriputto āyasmantaṃ lakuṇḍakabhaddiyaṃ sekhaṃ maññamāno bhiyyoso mattāya anekapariyāyena dhammiyā kathāya sandasseti samādapeti samuttejeti sampahaṃseti.
609Addasā kho bhagavā āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ āyasmantaṃ lakuṇḍakabhaddiyaṃ sekhaṃ maññamānaṃ bhiyyoso mattāya anekapariyāyena dhammiyā kathāya sandassentaṃ samādapentaṃ samuttejentaṃ sampahaṃsentaṃ.
610Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi— 
611
“Acchecchi vaṭṭaṃ byagā nirāsaṃ,
Visukkhā saritā na sandati;
Chinnaṃ vaṭṭaṃ na vattati,
Esevanto dukkhassā”ti. (75)
612Dutiyaṃ.
