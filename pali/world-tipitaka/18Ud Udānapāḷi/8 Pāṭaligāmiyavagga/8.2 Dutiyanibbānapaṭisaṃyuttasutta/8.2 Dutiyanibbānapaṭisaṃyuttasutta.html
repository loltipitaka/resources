8.2 Dutiyanibbānapaṭisaṃyuttasutta
Pāṭaligāmiyavagga
Dutiyanibbānapaṭisaṃyuttasutta
72.
665Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā bhikkhū nibbānapaṭisaṃyuttāya dhammiyā kathāya sandasseti samādapeti samuttejeti sampahaṃseti. Tedha bhikkhū aṭṭhiṃ katvā manasi katvā sabbaṃ cetaso samannāharitvā ohitasotā dhammaṃ suṇanti.
666Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi— 
667
“Duddasaṃ anataṃ nāma,
na hi saccaṃ sudassanaṃ;
Paṭividdhā taṇhā jānato,
passato natthi kiñcanan”ti. (84)
668Dutiyaṃ.
