1.7 Ajakalāpakasutta
Bodhivagga
Ajakalāpakasutta
7.
33Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā pāvāyaṃ viharati ajakalāpake cetiye, ajakalāpakassa yakkhassa bhavane. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā rattandhakāratimisāyaṃ abbhokāse nisinno hoti; devo ca ekamekaṃ phusāyati. Atha kho ajakalāpako yakkho bhagavato bhayaṃ chambhitattaṃ lomahaṃsaṃ uppādetukāmo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavato avidūre tikkhattuṃ “akkulo pakkulo”ti akkulapakkulikaṃ akāsi—  “eso te, samaṇa, pisāco”ti.
34Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi— 
35
“Yadā sakesu dhammesu,
pāragū hoti brāhmaṇo;
Atha etaṃ pisācañca,
pakkulañcātivattatī”ti. (7)
36Sattamaṃ.
