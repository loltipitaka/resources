6.1.8 Taṇhāsutta
Kilesasaṃyutta
Kilesavagga
Taṇhāsutta
329.
1107Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Yo, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yo saddataṇhāya…  yo gandhataṇhāya…  yo rasataṇhāya…  yo phoṭṭhabbataṇhāya…  yo dhammataṇhāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno…pe…  abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū”ti.
1108Aṭṭhamaṃ.
