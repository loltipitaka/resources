1.2.3.4 Arahantasutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Majjhimapaṇṇāsaka
Khajjanīyavagga
Arahantasutta
76.
385Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ. Yadaniccaṃ taṃ dukkhaṃ; yaṃ dukkhaṃ tadanattā; yadanattā taṃ ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ. Vedanā…  saññā…  saṅkhārā…  viññāṇaṃ aniccaṃ. Yadaniccaṃ taṃ dukkhaṃ; yaṃ dukkhaṃ tadanattā; yadanattā taṃ ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ.
386Evaṃ passaṃ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako rūpasmimpi nibbindati, vedanāyapi…  saññāyapi…  saṅkhāresupi…  viññāṇasmimpi nibbindati. Nibbindaṃ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati. Vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānāti. Yāvatā, bhikkhave, sattāvāsā, yāvatā bhavaggaṃ, ete aggā, ete seṭṭhā lokasmiṃ yadidaṃ arahanto”ti.
387Idamavoca bhagavā. Idaṃ vatvāna sugato athāparaṃ etadavoca satthā— 
388
“Sukhino vata arahanto,
taṇhā tesaṃ na vijjati;
Asmimāno samucchinno,
mohajālaṃ padālitaṃ.
389
Anejaṃ te anuppattā,
cittaṃ tesaṃ anāvilaṃ;
Loke anupalittā te,
brahmabhūtā anāsavā.
390
Pañcakkhandhe pariññāya,
satta saddhammagocarā;
Pasaṃsiyā sappurisā,
puttā buddhassa orasā.
391
Sattaratanasampannā,
Tīsu sikkhāsu sikkhitā;
Anuvicaranti mahāvīrā,
Pahīnabhayabheravā.
392
Dasahaṅgehi sampannā,
mahānāgā samāhitā;
Ete kho seṭṭhā lokasmiṃ,
taṇhā tesaṃ na vijjati.
393
Asekhañāṇamuppannaṃ,
antimoyaṃ samussayo;
Yo sāro brahmacariyassa,
tasmiṃ aparapaccayā.
394
Vidhāsu na vikampanti,
vippamuttā punabbhavā;
Dantabhūmimanuppattā,
te loke vijitāvino.
395
Uddhaṃ tiriyaṃ apācīnaṃ,
nandī tesaṃ na vijjati;
Nadanti te sīhanādaṃ,
buddhā loke anuttarā”ti.
396Catutthaṃ.
