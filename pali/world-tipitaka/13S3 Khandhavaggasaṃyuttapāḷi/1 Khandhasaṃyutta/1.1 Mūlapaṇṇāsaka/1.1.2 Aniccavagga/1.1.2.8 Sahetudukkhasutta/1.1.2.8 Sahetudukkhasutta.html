1.1.2.8 Sahetudukkhasutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Mūlapaṇṇāsaka
Aniccavagga
Sahetudukkhasutta
19.
112Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ. Yopi hetu yopi paccayo rūpassa uppādāya, sopi dukkho. Dukkhasambhūtaṃ, bhikkhave, rūpaṃ kuto sukhaṃ bhavissati. Vedanā dukkhā…  saññā dukkhā…  saṅkhārā dukkhā…  viññāṇaṃ dukkhaṃ. Yopi hetu yopi paccayo viññāṇassa uppādāya, sopi dukkho. Dukkhasambhūtaṃ, bhikkhave, viññāṇaṃ kuto sukhaṃ bhavissati. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.
113Aṭṭhamaṃ.
