3.1.14 Aññaṃjīvaṃaññaṃsarīraṃsutta
Diṭṭhisaṃyutta
Sotāpattivagga
Aññaṃjīvaṃaññaṃsarīraṃsutta
219.
963Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘aññaṃ jīvaṃ aññaṃ sarīran’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.
964Cuddasamaṃ.
