3.1.12 Anantavāsutta
Diṭṭhisaṃyutta
Sotāpattivagga
Anantavāsutta
217.
959Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘anantavā loko’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.
960Dvādasamaṃ.
