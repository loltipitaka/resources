3.2.19 Rūpīattāsutta
Diṭṭhisaṃyutta
Dutiyagamanavagga
Rūpīattāsutta
242.
990Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe… 
991“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṃ upādāya, rūpaṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṃ maraṇā’ti. Vedanāya sati…pe…  saññāya sati…  saṅkhāresu sati…  viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṃ upādāya, viññāṇaṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṃ maraṇā’ti.
992Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti? “Aniccaṃ, bhante” …pe…  vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? “No hetaṃ, bhante”. “Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṃ upādāya, dukkhaṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? “Vedanā…pe…  “no hetaṃ, bhante”. “Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṃ upādāya, dukkhaṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṃ maraṇā’”ti.
993Ekūnavīsatimaṃ.
