4.1.7 Rūpasañcetanāsutta
Okkantasaṃyutta
Cakkhuvagga
Rūpasañcetanāsutta
308.
1055Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpasañcetanā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddasañcetanā…  gandhasañcetanā…  rasasañcetanā…  phoṭṭhabbasañcetanā…  dhammasañcetanā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī. Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṃ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṃ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī…pe…  sambodhiparāyano’”ti.
1056Sattamaṃ.
