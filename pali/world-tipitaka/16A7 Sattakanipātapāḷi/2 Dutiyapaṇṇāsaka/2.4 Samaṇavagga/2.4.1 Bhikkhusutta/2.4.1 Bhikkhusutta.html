2.4.1 Bhikkhusutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Samaṇavagga
Bhikkhusutta
85.
632“Sattannaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ bhinnattā bhikkhu hoti. Katamesaṃ sattannaṃ? Sakkāyadiṭṭhi bhinnā hoti, vicikicchā bhinnā hoti, sīlabbataparāmāso bhinno hoti, rāgo bhinno hoti, doso bhinno hoti, moho bhinno hoti, māno bhinno hoti. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, sattannaṃ dhammānaṃ bhinnattā bhikkhu hotī”ti.
633Paṭhamaṃ.
