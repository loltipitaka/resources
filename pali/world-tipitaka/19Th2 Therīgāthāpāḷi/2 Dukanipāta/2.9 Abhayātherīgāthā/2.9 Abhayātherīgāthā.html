2.9 Abhayātherīgāthā
Dukanipāta
Abhayātherīgāthā
35.
63
“Abhaye bhiduro kāyo,
yattha satā puthujjanā;
Nikkhipissāmimaṃ dehaṃ,
sampajānā satīmatī.
36.
64
Bahūhi dukkhadhammehi,
appamādaratāya me;
Taṇhakkhayo anuppatto,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.
