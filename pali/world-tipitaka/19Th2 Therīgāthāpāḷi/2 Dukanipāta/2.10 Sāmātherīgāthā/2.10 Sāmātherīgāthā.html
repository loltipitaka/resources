2.10 Sāmātherīgāthā
Dukanipāta
Sāmātherīgāthā
37.
66
“Catukkhattuṃ pañcakkhattuṃ,
vihārā upanikkhamiṃ;
Aladdhā cetaso santiṃ,
citte avasavattinī;
Tassā me aṭṭhamī ratti,
yato taṇhā samūhatā.
38.
67
Bahūhi dukkhadhammehi,
appamādaratāya me;
Taṇhakkhayo anuppatto,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.
68…  Sāmā therī… .
69Dukanipāto niṭṭhito.
