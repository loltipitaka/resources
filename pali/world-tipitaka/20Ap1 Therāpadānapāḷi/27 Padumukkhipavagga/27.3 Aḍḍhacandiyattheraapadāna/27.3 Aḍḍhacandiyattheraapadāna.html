27.3 Aḍḍhacandiyattheraapadāna
Padumukkhipavagga
Aḍḍhacandiyattheraapadāna
10.
3171
“Tissassa kho bhagavato,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Aḍḍhacandaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dharaṇīruhapādape.
11.
3172
Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ candamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
12.
3173
Pañcavīse ito kappe,
devalo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
13.
3174
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3175Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aḍḍhacandiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3176Aḍḍhacandiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
