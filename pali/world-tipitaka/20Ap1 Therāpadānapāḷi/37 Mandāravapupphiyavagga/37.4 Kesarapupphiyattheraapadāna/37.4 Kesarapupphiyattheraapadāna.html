37.4 Kesarapupphiyattheraapadāna
Mandāravapupphiyavagga
Kesarapupphiyattheraapadāna
12.
4050
“Vijjādharo tadā āsiṃ,
himavantamhi pabbate;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
caṅkamantaṃ mahāyasaṃ.
13.
4051
Tīṇi kesarapupphāni,
sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;
Upasaṅkamma sambuddhaṃ,
vessabhuṃ abhipūjayiṃ.
14.
4052
Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
15.
4053
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
4054Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kesarapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
4055Kesarapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
