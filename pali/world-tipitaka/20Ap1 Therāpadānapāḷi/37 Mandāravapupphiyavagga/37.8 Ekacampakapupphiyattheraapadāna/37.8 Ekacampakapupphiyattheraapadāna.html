37.8 Ekacampakapupphiyattheraapadāna
Mandāravapupphiyavagga
Ekacampakapupphiyattheraapadāna
27.
4073
“Upasanto ca sambuddho,
vasatī pabbatantare;
Ekacampakamādāya,
upagacchiṃ naruttamaṃ.
28.
4074
Pasannacitto sumano,
paccekamunimuttamaṃ;
Ubhohatthehi paggayha,
pūjayiṃ aparājitaṃ.
29.
4075
Pañcasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
30.
4076
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
4077Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekacampakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
4078Ekacampakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
