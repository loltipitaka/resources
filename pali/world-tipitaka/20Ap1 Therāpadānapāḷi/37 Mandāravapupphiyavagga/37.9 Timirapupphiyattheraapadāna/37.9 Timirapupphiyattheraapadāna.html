37.9 Timirapupphiyattheraapadāna
Mandāravapupphiyavagga
Timirapupphiyattheraapadāna
31.
4079
“Candabhāgānadītīre,
anusotaṃ vajāmahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ.
32.
4080
Pasannacitto sumano,
paccekamunimuttamaṃ;
Gahetvā timiraṃ pupphaṃ,
matthake okiriṃ ahaṃ.
33.
4081
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
34.
4082
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
4083Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā timirapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
