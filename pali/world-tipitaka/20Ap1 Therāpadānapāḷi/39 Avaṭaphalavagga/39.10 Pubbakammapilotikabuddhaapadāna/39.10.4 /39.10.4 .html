39.10.4
Avaṭaphalavagga
Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna
78.
4272
Vemātubhātaraṃ pubbe,
dhanahetu haniṃ ahaṃ;
Pakkhipiṃ giriduggasmiṃ,
silāya ca apiṃsayiṃ.
79.
4273
Tena kammavipākena,
devadatto silaṃ khipi;
Aṅguṭṭhaṃ piṃsayī pāde,
mama pāsāṇasakkharā.
