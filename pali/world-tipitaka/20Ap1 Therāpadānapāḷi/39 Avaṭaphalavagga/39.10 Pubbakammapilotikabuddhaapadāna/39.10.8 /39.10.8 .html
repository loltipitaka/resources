39.10.8
Avaṭaphalavagga
Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna
86.
4280
Ahaṃ kevaṭṭagāmasmiṃ,
ahuṃ kevaṭṭadārako;
Macchake ghātite disvā,
janayiṃ somanassakaṃ.
87.
4281
Tena kammavipākena,
sīsadukkhaṃ ahū mama;
Sabbe sakkā ca haññiṃsu,
yadā hani viṭaṭūbho.
