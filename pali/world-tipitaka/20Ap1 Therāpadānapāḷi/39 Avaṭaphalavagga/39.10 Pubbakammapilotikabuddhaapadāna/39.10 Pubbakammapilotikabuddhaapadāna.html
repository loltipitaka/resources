39.10 Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna
Avaṭaphalavagga
Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna
64.
4258
Anotattasarāsanne,
ramaṇīye silātale;
Nānāratanapajjote,
nānāgandhavanantare.
Avaṭaphalavagga
Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna
65.
4259
Mahatā bhikkhusaṃghena,
pareto lokanāyako;
Āsīno byākarī tattha,
pubbakammāni attano.
Avaṭaphalavagga

66.
4260
“Suṇātha bhikkhavo mayhaṃ,
yaṃ kammaṃ pakataṃ mayā;
Pilotikassa kammassa,
buddhattepi vipaccati.
