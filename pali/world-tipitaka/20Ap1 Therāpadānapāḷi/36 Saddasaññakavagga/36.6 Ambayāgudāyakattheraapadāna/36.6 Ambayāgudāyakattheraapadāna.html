36.6 Ambayāgudāyakattheraapadāna
Saddasaññakavagga
Ambayāgudāyakattheraapadāna
22.
4001
“Sataraṃsī nāma sambuddho,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vuṭṭhahitvā samādhimhā,
bhikkhāya mamupāgami.
23.
4002
Paccekabuddhaṃ disvāna,
ambayāguṃ adāsahaṃ;
Vippasannamanaṃ tassa,
vippasannena cetasā.
24.
4003
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ambayāguyidaṃ phalaṃ.
25.
4004
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
4005Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ambayāgudāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
4006Ambayāgudāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
