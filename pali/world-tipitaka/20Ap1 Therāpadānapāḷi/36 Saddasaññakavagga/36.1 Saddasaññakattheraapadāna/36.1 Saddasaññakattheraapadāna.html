36.1 Saddasaññakattheraapadāna
Saddasaññakavagga
Saddasaññakattheraapadāna
1.
3970
“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
devasaṅghapurakkhataṃ.
2.
3971
Catusaccaṃ pakāsentaṃ,
uddharantaṃ mahājanaṃ;
Assosiṃ madhuraṃ vācaṃ,
karavīkarudopamaṃ.
3.
3972
Brahmasarassa munino,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Ghose cittaṃ pasādetvā,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.
4.
3973
Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pasādassa idaṃ phalaṃ.
5.
3974
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3975Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3976Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
