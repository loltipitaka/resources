15.8 Phalakadāyakattheraapadāna
Chattavagga
Phalakadāyakattheraapadāna
37.
2328
“Yānakāro pure āsiṃ,
dārukamme susikkhito;
Candanaṃ phalakaṃ katvā,
adāsiṃ lokabandhuno.
38.
2329
Pabhāsati idaṃ byamhaṃ,
suvaṇṇassa sunimmitaṃ;
Hatthiyānaṃ assayānaṃ,
dibbayānaṃ upaṭṭhitaṃ.
39.
2330
Pāsādā sivikā ceva,
Nibbattanti yadicchakaṃ;
Akkhobhaṃ ratanaṃ mayhaṃ,
Phalakassa idaṃ phalaṃ.
40.
2331
Ekanavutito kappe,
phalakaṃ yamahaṃ dadiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phalakassa idaṃ phalaṃ.
41.
2332
Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
caturo nimmitāvhayā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.
42.
2333
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2334Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phalakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2335Phalakadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
