46.4 Tiṇukkadhāriyattheraapadāna
Jagatidāyakavagga
Tiṇukkadhāriyattheraapadāna
22.
5906
“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Pasannacitto sumano,
tayo ukke adhārayiṃ.
23.
5907
Satasahassito kappe,
sohaṃ ukkamadhārayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ukkadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
24.
5908
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
25.
5909
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
26.
5910
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
5911Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇukkadhāriyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
5912Tiṇukkadhāriyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
