14.7 Atthasandassakattheraapadāna
Sobhitavagga
Atthasandassakattheraapadāna
47.
2240
“Visālamāḷe āsīno,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Khīṇāsavaṃ balappattaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhataṃ.
48.
2241
Satasahassā tevijjā,
chaḷabhiññā mahiddhikā;
Parivārenti sambuddhaṃ,
ko disvā nappasīdati.
49.
2242
Ñāṇe upanidhā yassa,
na vijjati sadevake;
Anantañāṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
ko disvā nappasīdati.
50.
2243
Dhammakāyañca dīpentaṃ,
kevalaṃ ratanākaraṃ;
Vikappetuṃ na sakkonti,
ko disvā nappasīdati.
51.
2244
Imāhi tīhi gāthāhi,
nāradovhayavacchalo;
Padumuttaraṃ thavitvāna,
sambuddhaṃ aparājitaṃ.
52.
2245
Tena cittappasādena,
buddhasanthavanena ca;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.
53.
2246
Ito tiṃsakappasate,
sumitto nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
54.
2247
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2248Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā atthasandassako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2249Atthasandassakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
