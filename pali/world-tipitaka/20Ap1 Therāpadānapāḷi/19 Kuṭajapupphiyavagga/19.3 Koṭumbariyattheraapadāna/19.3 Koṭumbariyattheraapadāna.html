19.3 Koṭumbariyattheraapadāna
Kuṭajapupphiyavagga
Koṭumbariyattheraapadāna
12.
2596
“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,
nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;
Appameyyaṃva udadhiṃ,
vitthataṃ dharaṇiṃ yathā.
13.
2597
Pūjitaṃ devasaṅghena,
nisabhājāniyaṃ yathā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
upāgacchiṃ naruttamaṃ.
14.
2598
Sattapupphāni paggayha,
koṭumbarasamākulaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.
15.
2599
Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
16.
2600
Ito vīsatikappamhi,
mahānelasanāmako;
Eko āsi mahātejo,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
17.
2601
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2602Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā koṭumbariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2603Koṭumbariyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
