19.6 Bodhiupaṭṭhākattheraapadāna
Kuṭajapupphiyavagga
Bodhiupaṭṭhākattheraapadāna
30.
2620
“Nagare rammavatiyā,
āsiṃ murajavādako;
Niccupaṭṭhānayuttomhi,
gatohaṃ bodhimuttamaṃ.
31.
2621
Sāyaṃ pātaṃ upaṭṭhitvā,
sukkamūlena codito;
Aṭṭhārasakappasate,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.
32.
2622
Pannarase kappasate,
ito āsiṃ janādhipo;
Murajo nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
33.
2623
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2624Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bodhiupaṭṭhāko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2625Bodhiupaṭṭhākattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
