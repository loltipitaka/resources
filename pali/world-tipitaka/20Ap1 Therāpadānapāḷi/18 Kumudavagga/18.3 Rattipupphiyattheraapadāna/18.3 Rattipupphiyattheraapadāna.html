18.3 Rattipupphiyattheraapadāna
Kumudavagga
Rattipupphiyattheraapadāna
13.
2526
“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Vipassiṃ addasaṃ buddhaṃ,
devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ.
14.
2527
Rattikaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
kuṭajaṃ dharaṇīruhaṃ;
Samūlaṃ paggahetvāna,
upanesiṃ mahesino.
15.
2528
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
16.
2529
Ito ca aṭṭhame kappe,
suppasannasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
rājāhosiṃ mahabbalo.
17.
2530
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2531Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā rattipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2532Rattipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
