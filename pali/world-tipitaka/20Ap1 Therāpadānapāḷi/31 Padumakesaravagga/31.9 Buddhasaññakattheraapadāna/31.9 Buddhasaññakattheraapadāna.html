31.9 Buddhasaññakattheraapadāna
Padumakesaravagga
Buddhasaññakattheraapadāna
43.
3490
“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Vanantaragataṃ santaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.
44.
3491
Addasaṃ supinantena,
siddhatthaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
sugatiṃ upapajjahaṃ.
45.
3492
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
46.
3493
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3494Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā buddhasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3495Buddhasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
