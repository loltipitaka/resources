31.8 Anulepadāyakattheraapadāna
Padumakesaravagga
Anulepadāyakattheraapadāna
39.
3484
“Atthadassissa munino,
addasaṃ sāvakaṃ ahaṃ;
Navakammaṃ karontassa,
sīmāya upagacchahaṃ.
40.
3485
Niṭṭhite navakamme ca,
anulepamadāsahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
puññakkhette anuttare.
41.
3486
Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
anulepassidaṃ phalaṃ.
42.
3487
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3488Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā anulepadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3489Anulepadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
