38.5 Sattapaṇṇiyattheraapadāna
Bodhivandanavagga
Sattapaṇṇiyattheraapadāna
28.
4131
“Sumano nāma sambuddho,
uppajji lokanāyako;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sattapaṇṇimapūjayiṃ.
29.
4132
Satasahassito kappe,
Sattapaṇṇimapūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
Sattapaṇṇipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
30.
4133
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
31.
4134
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
32.
4135
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
4136Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattapaṇṇiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
4137Sattapaṇṇiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
