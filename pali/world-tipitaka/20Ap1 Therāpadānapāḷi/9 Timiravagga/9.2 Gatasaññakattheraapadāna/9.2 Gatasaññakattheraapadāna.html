9.2 Gatasaññakattheraapadāna
Timiravagga
Gatasaññakattheraapadāna
10.
1697
“Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Avandiṃ satthuno pāde,
vippasannena cetasā.
11.
1698
Sattanaṅgalakīpupphe,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;
Tissaṃ buddhaṃ samuddissa,
anantaguṇasāgaraṃ.
12.
1699
Sugatānugataṃ maggaṃ,
pūjetvā haṭṭhamānaso;
Añjaliñca tadākāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.
13.
1700
Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
14.
1701
Ito aṭṭhamake kappe,
tayo aggisikhā ahu;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.
15.
1702
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
1703Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gatasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
1704Gatasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
