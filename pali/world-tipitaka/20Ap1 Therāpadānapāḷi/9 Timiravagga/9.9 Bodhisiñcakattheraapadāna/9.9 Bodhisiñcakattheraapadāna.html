9.9 Bodhisiñcakattheraapadāna
Timiravagga
Bodhisiñcakattheraapadāna
46.
1747
“Vipassissa bhagavato,
mahābodhimaho ahu;
Pabbajjupagato santo,
upagacchiṃ ahaṃ tadā.
47.
1748
Kusumodakamādāya,
bodhiyā okiriṃ ahaṃ;
Mocayissati no mutto,
nibbāpessati nibbuto.
48.
1749
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ bodhimabhisiñcayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhisiñcāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
49.
1750
Tettiṃse vattamānamhi,
kappe āsuṃ janādhipā;
Udakasecanā nāma,
aṭṭhete cakkavattino.
50.
1751
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
1752Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bodhisiñcako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
1753Bodhisiñcakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
