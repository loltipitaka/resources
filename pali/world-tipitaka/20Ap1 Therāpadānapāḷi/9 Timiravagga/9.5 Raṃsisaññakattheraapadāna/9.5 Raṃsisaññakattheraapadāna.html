9.5 Raṃsisaññakattheraapadāna
Timiravagga
Raṃsisaññakattheraapadāna
30.
1723
“Pabbate himavantamhi,
vāsaṃ kappesahaṃ pure;
Ajinuttaravāsohaṃ,
vasāmi pabbatantare.
31.
1724
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Sataraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Vanantaragataṃ disvā,
Sālarājaṃva pupphitaṃ.
32.
1725
Raṃsyā cittaṃ pasādetvā,
vipassissa mahesino;
Paggayha añjaliṃ vandiṃ,
sirasā ukkuṭī ahaṃ.
33.
1726
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
raṃsisaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
34.
1727
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
1728Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā raṃsisaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
1729Raṃsisaññakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
