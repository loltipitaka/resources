23.6 Aṅkolapupphiyattheraapadāna
Ālambaṇadāyakavagga
Aṅkolapupphiyattheraapadāna
29.
2937
“Nārado iti me nāmaṃ,
kassapo iti maṃ vidū;
Addasaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
vipassiṃ devasakkataṃ.
30.
2938
Anubyañjanadharaṃ buddhaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Aṅkolapupphaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.
31.
2939
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
32.
2940
Catusattatito kappe,
romaso nāma khattiyo;
Āmukkamālābharaṇo,
sayoggabalavāhano.
33.
2941
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2942Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aṅkolapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2943Aṅkolapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
