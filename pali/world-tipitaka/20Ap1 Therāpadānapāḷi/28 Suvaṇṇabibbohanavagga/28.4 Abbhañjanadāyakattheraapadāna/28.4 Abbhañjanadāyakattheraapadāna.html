28.4 Abbhañjanadāyakattheraapadāna
Suvaṇṇabibbohanavagga
Abbhañjanadāyakattheraapadāna
14.
3242
“Koṇḍaññassa bhagavato,
vītarāgassa tādino;
Ākāsasamacittassa,
nippapañcassa jhāyino.
15.
3243
Sabbamohātivattassa,
sabbalokahitesino;
Abbhañjanaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dvipadindassa tādino.
16.
3244
Aparimeyye ito kappe,
abbhañjanamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
abbhañjanassidaṃ phalaṃ.
17.
3245
Ito pannarase kappe,
cirappo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
18.
3246
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3247Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā abbhañjanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3248Abbhañjanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
