28.3 Caṅkoṭakiyattheraapadāna
Suvaṇṇabibbohanavagga
Caṅkoṭakiyattheraapadāna
10.
3236
“Mahāsamuddaṃ nissāya,
vasatī pabbatantare;
Paccuggantvāna katvāna,
caṅkoṭaka madāsahaṃ.
11.
3237
Siddhatthassa mahesino,
sabbasattānukampino;
Pupphacaṅkoṭakaṃ datvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.
12.
3238
Catunnavutito kappe,
caṅkoṭakamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
caṅkoṭakassidaṃ phalaṃ.
13.
3239
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3240Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā caṅkoṭakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3241Caṅkoṭakiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
