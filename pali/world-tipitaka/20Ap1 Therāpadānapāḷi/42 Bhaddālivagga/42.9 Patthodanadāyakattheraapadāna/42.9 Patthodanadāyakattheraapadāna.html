42.9 Patthodanadāyakattheraapadāna
Bhaddālivagga
Patthodanadāyakattheraapadāna
170.
5435
“Vanacārī pure āsiṃ,
satataṃ vanakammiko;
Patthodanaṃ gahetvāna,
kammantaṃ agamāsahaṃ.
171.
5436
Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ;
Vanā piṇḍāya nikkhantaṃ,
disvā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.
172.
5437
Parakammāyane yutto,
puññañca me na vijjati;
Ayaṃ patthodano atthi,
bhojayissāmahaṃ muniṃ.
173.
5438
Patthodanaṃ gahetvāna,
sayambhussa adāsahaṃ;
Mama nijjhāyamānassa,
paribhuñji tadā muni.
174.
5439
Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.
175.
5440
Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.
176.
5441
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Sukhito yasavā homi,
patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.
177.
5442
Bhavābhave saṃsaranto,
labhāmi amitaṃ dhanaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.
178.
5443
Nadīsotapaṭibhāgā,
bhogā nibbattare mama;
Parimetuṃ na sakkomi,
patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.
179.
5444
Imaṃ khāda imaṃ bhuñja,
imamhi sayane saya;
Tenāhaṃ sukhito homi,
patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.
180.
5445
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.
181.
5446
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
182.
5447
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
183.
5448
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
5449Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā patthodanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
5450Patthodanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
