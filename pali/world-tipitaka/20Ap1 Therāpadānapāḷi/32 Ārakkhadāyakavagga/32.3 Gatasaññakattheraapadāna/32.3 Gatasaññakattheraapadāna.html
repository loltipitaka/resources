32.3 Gatasaññakattheraapadāna
Ārakkhadāyakavagga
Gatasaññakattheraapadāna
10.
3519
“Ākāseva padaṃ natthi,
ambare anilañjase;
Siddhatthaṃ jinamaddakkhiṃ,
gacchantaṃ tidivaṅgaṇe.
11.
3520
Anileneritaṃ disvā,
sammāsambuddhacīvaraṃ;
Vitti mamāhu tāvade,
disvāna gamanaṃ muniṃ.
12.
3521
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
13.
3522
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3523Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gatasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3524Gatasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
