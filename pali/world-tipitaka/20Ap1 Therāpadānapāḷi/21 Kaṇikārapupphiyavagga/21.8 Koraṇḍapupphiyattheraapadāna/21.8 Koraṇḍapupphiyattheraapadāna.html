21.8 Koraṇḍapupphiyattheraapadāna
Kaṇikārapupphiyavagga
Koraṇḍapupphiyattheraapadāna
35.
2798
“Akkantañca padaṃ disvā,
cakkālaṅkārabhūsitaṃ;
Padenānupadaṃ yanto,
vipassissa mahesino.
36.
2799
Koraṇḍaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
samūlaṃ pūjitaṃ mayā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
avandiṃ padamuttamaṃ.
37.
2800
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
38.
2801
Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
eko vītamalo ahuṃ;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
39.
2802
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2803Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā koraṇḍapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2804Koraṇḍapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
