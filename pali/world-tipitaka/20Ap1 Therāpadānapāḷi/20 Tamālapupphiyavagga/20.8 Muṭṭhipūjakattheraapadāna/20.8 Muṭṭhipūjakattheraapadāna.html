20.8 Muṭṭhipūjakattheraapadāna
Tamālapupphiyavagga
Muṭṭhipūjakattheraapadāna
42.
2721
“Sumedho nāma bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Pacchime anukampāya,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.
43.
2722
Tassa caṅkamamānassa,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Girinelassa pupphānaṃ,
muṭṭhiṃ buddhassa ropayiṃ.
44.
2723
Tena cittappasādena,
sukkamūlena codito;
Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.
45.
2724
Tevīsatikappasate,
sunelo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
eko āsiṃ mahabbalo.
46.
2725
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2726Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā muṭṭhipūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2727Muṭṭhipūjakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
