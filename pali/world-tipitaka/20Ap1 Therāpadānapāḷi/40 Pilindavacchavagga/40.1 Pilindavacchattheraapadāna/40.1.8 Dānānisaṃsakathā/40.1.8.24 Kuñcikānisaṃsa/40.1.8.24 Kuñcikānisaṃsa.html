40.1.8.24 Kuñcikānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Kuñcikānisaṃsa
154.
4451
Kuñcike sugate datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Dhammadvāravivaraṇaṃ,
labhāmi ñāṇakuñcikaṃ.
