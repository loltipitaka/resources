40.1.8.42 Dadhiānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Dadhiānisaṃsa
197.
4494
Dadhiṃ datvāna sampannaṃ,
buddhe saṃghe gaṇuttame;
Bhuñjāmi amataṃ bhattaṃ,
varaṃ kāyagatāsatiṃ.
