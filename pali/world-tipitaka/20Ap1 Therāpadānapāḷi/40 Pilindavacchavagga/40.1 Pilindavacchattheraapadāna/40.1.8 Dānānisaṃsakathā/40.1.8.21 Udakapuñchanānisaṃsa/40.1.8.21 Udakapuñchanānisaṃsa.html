40.1.8.21 Udakapuñchanānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Udakapuñchanānisaṃsa
147.
4444
Udakapuñchanacoḷe,
datvā buddhe gaṇuttame;
Pañcānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
148.
4445
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo virajo,
sappabhāso patāpavā;
Siniddhaṃ hoti me gattaṃ,
rajojallaṃ na limpati;
Ime guṇe paṭilabhe,
tassa nissandato ahaṃ.
