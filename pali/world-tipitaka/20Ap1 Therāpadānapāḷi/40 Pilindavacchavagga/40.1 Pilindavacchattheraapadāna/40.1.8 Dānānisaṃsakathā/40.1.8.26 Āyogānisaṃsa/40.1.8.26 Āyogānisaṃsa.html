40.1.8.26 Āyogānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Āyogānisaṃsa
156.
4453
Āyoge sugate datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Pañcānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
157.
4454
Samādhīsu na kampāmi,
vasī homi samādhisu;
Abhejjapariso homi,
ādeyyavacano sadā;
Jāyati bhogasampatti,
bhave saṃsarato mama.
