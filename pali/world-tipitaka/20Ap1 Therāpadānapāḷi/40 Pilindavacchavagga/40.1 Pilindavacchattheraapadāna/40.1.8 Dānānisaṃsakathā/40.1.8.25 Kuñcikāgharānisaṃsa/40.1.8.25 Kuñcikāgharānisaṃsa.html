40.1.8.25 Kuñcikāgharānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Kuñcikāgharānisaṃsa
155.
4452
Kuñcikānaṃ ghare datvā,
buddhe saṃghe gaṇuttame;
Dvānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama;
Appakodho anāyāso,
saṃsaranto bhave ahaṃ.
