40.1.8.44 Rasānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Rasānisaṃsa
199.
4496
Yathābhūtaṃ rasaṃ datvā,
buddhe saṃghe gaṇuttame;
Caturo phale anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
