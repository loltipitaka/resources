40.1.8.32 Saṇḍāsānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Saṇḍāsānisaṃsa
169.
4466
Saṇḍāse sugate datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Kilesabhañjanaṃ ñāṇaṃ,
labhāmi atulaṃ suciṃ.
