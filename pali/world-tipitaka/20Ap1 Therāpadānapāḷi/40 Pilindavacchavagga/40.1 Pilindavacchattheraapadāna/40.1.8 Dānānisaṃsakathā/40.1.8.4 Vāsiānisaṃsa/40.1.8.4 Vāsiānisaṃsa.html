40.1.8.4 Vāsiānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Vāsiānisaṃsa
103.
4400
Cittabandhanasambaddhā,
sukatā vāsiyo bahū;
Datvāna buddhaseṭṭhassa,
saṃghassa ca tathevahaṃ.
104.
4401
Aṭṭhānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama;
Sūro homavisārī ca,
vesārajjesu pāramī.
105.
4402
Dhitivīriyavā homi,
paggahītamano sadā;
Kilesacchedanaṃ ñāṇaṃ,
sukhumaṃ atulaṃ suciṃ;
Sabbattha paṭilabhāmi,
tassa nissandato ahaṃ.
