13.9 Gaṇṭhipupphiyattheraapadāna
Sereyyavagga
Gaṇṭhipupphiyattheraapadāna
91.
2160
“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sambuddho,
vipassī dakkhiṇāraho;
Purakkhato sāvakehi,
ārāmā abhinikkhami.
92.
2161
Disvānahaṃ buddhaseṭṭhaṃ,
sabbaññuṃ tamanāsakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gaṇṭhipupphaṃ apūjayiṃ.
93.
2162
Tena cittappasādena,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
puna vandiṃ tathāgataṃ.
94.
2163
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
95.
2164
Ekatālīsito kappe,
caraṇo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
96.
2165
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2166Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gaṇṭhipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2167Gaṇṭhipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
