40.
115Kenaci viññeyyaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakenaci viññeyyo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.
116Kenaci viññeyyaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakenaci viññeyyo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.
117Kenaci viññeyyaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nakenaci viññeyyo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.
118Cūḷantaradukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
