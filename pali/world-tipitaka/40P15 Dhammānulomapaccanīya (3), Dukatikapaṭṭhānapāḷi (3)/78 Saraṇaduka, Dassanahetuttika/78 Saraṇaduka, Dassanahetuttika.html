247Saraṇaṃ dassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  sahajāte tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
248Saraṇaṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  sahajāte tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
249Araṇaṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
