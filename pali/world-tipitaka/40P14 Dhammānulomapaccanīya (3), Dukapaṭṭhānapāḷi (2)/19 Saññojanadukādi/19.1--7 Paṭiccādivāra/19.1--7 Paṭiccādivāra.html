Paṭiccādivāra

77Saññojanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nosaññojano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  ganthaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nogantho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  oghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noogho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  yogaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noyogo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  nīvaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nonīvaraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. 78Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.
