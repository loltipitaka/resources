17.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Āsavaāsavasampayuttaduka
Paṭiccādivāra
20.
68Āsavañceva āsavasampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
69Āsavasampayuttañceva no ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavavippayutto ceva nano ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
70Āsavañceva āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañceva no ca āsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
71Hetuyā nava. (Sabbattha nava.)
