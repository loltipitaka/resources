915Sanidassanasappaṭigho nahetu dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa nahetussa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
916Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā nava, anantare ekaṃ, samanantare ekaṃ, sahajāte ekavīsa, aññamaññe cha, nissaye ekavīsa, upanissaye tīṇi, purejāte cha, pacchājāte satta, āsevane ekaṃ, kamme vipāke āhāre satta, indriye nava, jhāne magge satta, sampayutte ekaṃ, vippayutte aṭṭha…pe…  avigate pañcavīsa. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
917Nahetuyā pañcavīsa, naārammaṇe ekavīsa. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
918Ārammaṇapaccayā nahetuyā tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
919Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
920(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvāraṃ, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
