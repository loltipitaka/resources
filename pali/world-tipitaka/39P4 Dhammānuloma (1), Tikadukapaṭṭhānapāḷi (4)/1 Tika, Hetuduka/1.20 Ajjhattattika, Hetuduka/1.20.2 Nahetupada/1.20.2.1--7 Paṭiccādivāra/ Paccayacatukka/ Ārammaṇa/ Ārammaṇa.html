868Ajjhatto nahetu dhammo ajjhattassa nahetussa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Ajjhatto nahetu dhammo bahiddhā nahetussa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (2)
