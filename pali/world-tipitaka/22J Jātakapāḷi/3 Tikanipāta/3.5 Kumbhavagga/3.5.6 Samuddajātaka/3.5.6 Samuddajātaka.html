3.5.6 Samuddajātaka
Tikanipāta
Kumbhavagga
Samuddajātaka
136.
858
“Ko nāyaṃ loṇatoyasmiṃ,
samantā paridhāvati;
Macche makare ca vāreti,
ūmīsu ca vihaññati”.
137.
859
“Anantapāyī sakuṇo,
atittoti disāsuto;
Samuddaṃ pātumicchāmi,
sāgaraṃ saritaṃ patiṃ”.
138.
860
“So ayaṃ hāyati ceva,
pūrate ca mahodadhi;
Nāssa nāyati pītanto,
apeyyo kira sāgaro”ti.
861Samuddajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
