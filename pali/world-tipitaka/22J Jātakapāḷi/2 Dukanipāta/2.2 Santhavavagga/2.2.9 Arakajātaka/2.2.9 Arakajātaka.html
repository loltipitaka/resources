2.2.9 Arakajātaka
Dukanipāta
Santhavavagga
Arakajātaka
37.
398
“Yo ve mettena cittena,
sabbalokānukampati;
Uddhaṃ adho ca tiriyaṃ,
appamāṇena sabbaso.
38.
399
Appamāṇaṃ hitaṃ cittaṃ,
paripuṇṇaṃ subhāvitaṃ;
Yaṃ pamāṇakataṃ kammaṃ,
na taṃ tatrāvasissatī”ti.
400Arakajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
