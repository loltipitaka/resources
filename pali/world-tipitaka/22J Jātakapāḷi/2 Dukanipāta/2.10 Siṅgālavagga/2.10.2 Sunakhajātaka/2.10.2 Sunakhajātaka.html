2.10.2 Sunakhajātaka
Dukanipāta
Siṅgālavagga
Sunakhajātaka
184.
638
“Bālo vatāyaṃ sunakho,
yo varattaṃ na khādati;
Bandhanā ca pamuñceyya,
asito ca gharaṃ vaje”.
185.
639
“Aṭṭhitaṃ me manasmiṃ me,
atho me hadaye kataṃ;
Kālañca paṭikaṅkhāmi,
yāva passupatū jano”ti.
