1.11.1 Belaṭṭhānikattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Ekādasamavagga
Belaṭṭhānikattheragāthā
101.
227
“Hitvā gihittaṃ anavositatto,
Mukhanaṅgalī odariko kusīto;
Mahāvarāhova nivāpapuṭṭho,
Punappunaṃ gabbhamupeti mando”ti.
