1.7.6 Meghiyattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Sattamavagga
Meghiyattheragāthā
66.
149
“Anusāsi mahāvīro,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Tassāhaṃ dhammaṃ sutvāna,
vihāsiṃ santike sato;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.
