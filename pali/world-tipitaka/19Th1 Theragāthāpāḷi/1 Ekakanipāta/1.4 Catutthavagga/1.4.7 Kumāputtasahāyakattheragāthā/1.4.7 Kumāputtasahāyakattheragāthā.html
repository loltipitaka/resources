1.4.7 Kumāputtasahāyakattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Catutthavagga
Kumāputtasahāyakattheragāthā
37.
84
“Nānājanapadaṃ yanti,
Vicarantā asaññatā;
Samādhiñca virādhenti,
Kiṃsu raṭṭhacariyā karissati;
Tasmā vineyya sārambhaṃ,
Jhāyeyya apurakkhato”ti.
85…  Kumāputtattherassa sahāyako thero… .
