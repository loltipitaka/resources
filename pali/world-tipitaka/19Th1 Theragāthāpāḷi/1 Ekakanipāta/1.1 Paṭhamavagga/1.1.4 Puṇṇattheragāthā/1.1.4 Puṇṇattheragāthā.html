1.1.4 Puṇṇattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Puṇṇattheragāthā
4.
11
“Sabbhireva samāsetha,
paṇḍitehatthadassibhi;
Atthaṃ mahantaṃ gambhīraṃ,
duddasaṃ nipuṇaṃ aṇuṃ;
Dhīrā samadhigacchanti,
appamattā vicakkhaṇā”ti.
12Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā puṇṇo mantāṇiputto thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
