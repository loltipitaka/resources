1.1.9 Pilindavacchattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Pilindavacchattheragāthā
9.
21
“Svāgataṃ na durāgataṃ,
Nayidaṃ dumantitaṃ mama;
Saṃvibhattesu dhammesu,
Yaṃ seṭṭhaṃ tadupāgamin”ti.
22Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pilindavaccho thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
