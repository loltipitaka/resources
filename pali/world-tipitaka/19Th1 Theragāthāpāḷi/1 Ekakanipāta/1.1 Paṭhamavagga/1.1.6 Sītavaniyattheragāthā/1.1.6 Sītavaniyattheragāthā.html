1.1.6 Sītavaniyattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Sītavaniyattheragāthā
6.
15
“Yo sītavanaṃ upagā bhikkhu,
Eko santusito samāhitatto;
Vijitāvī apetalomahaṃso,
Rakkhaṃ kāyagatāsatiṃ dhitimā”ti.
16Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sītavaniyo thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
