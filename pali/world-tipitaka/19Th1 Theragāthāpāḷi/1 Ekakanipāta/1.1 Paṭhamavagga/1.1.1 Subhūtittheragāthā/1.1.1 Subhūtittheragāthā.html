1.1.1 Subhūtittheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Subhūtittheragāthā
1.
5
“Channā me kuṭikā sukhā nivātā,
Vassa deva yathāsukhaṃ;
Cittaṃ me susamāhitaṃ vimuttaṃ,
Ātāpī viharāmi vassa devā”ti.
6Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā subhūtitthero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
