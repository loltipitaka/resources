2.3.3 Sobhitattheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Tatiyavagga
Sobhitattheragāthā
165.
344
“Satimā paññavā bhikkhu,
āraddhabalavīriyo;
Pañca kappasatānāhaṃ,
ekarattiṃ anussariṃ.
166.
345
Cattāro satipaṭṭhāne,
satta aṭṭha ca bhāvayaṃ;
Pañca kappasatānāhaṃ,
ekarattiṃ anussarin”ti.
346…  Sobhito thero… .
