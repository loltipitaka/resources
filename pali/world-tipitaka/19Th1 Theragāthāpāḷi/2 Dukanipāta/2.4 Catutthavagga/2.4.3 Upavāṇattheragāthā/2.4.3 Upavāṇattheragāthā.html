Dukanipāta
Catutthavagga
Upavāṇattheragāthā

376
“Arahaṃ sugato loke,
vātehābādhiko muni;
Sace uṇhodakaṃ atthi,
munino dehi brāhmaṇa.

377
Pūjito pūjaneyyānaṃ,
sakkareyyāna sakkato;
Apacitopaceyyānaṃ,
tassa icchāmi hātave”ti.
