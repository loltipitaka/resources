2.4.1 Migasirattheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Catutthavagga
Migasirattheragāthā
181.
370
“Yato ahaṃ pabbajito,
sammāsambuddhasāsane;
Vimuccamāno uggacchiṃ,
kāmadhātuṃ upaccagaṃ.
182.
371
Brahmuno pekkhamānassa,
tato cittaṃ vimucci me;
Akuppā me vimuttīti,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayā”ti.
