Dukanipāta

“Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,
Piyarūpe manorame;
Saddhāya gharā nikkhamma,
Dukkhassantakaro bhave.

392
Nābhinandāmi maraṇaṃ,
nābhinandāmi jīvitaṃ;

sampajāno patissato”ti.
