2.4.2.10.2 Cittaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittaduka
1527.
2238Katame dhammā cittā? Cakkhuviññāṇaṃ, sotaviññāṇaṃ, ghānaviññāṇaṃ, jivhāviññāṇaṃ, kāyaviññāṇaṃ, manodhātu, manoviññāṇadhātu—  ime dhammā cittā.
1528.
2239Katame dhammā no cittā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittā.
