2.4.2.10.9 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
1541.
2252Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā.
1542.
2253Katame dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā.
