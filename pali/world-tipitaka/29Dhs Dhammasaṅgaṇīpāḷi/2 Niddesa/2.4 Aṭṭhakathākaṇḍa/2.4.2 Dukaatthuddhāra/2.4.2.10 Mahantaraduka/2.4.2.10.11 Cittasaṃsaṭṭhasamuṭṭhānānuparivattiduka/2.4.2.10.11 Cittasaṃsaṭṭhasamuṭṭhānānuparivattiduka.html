2.4.2.10.11 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka
1545.
2256Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.
1546.
2257Katame dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.
