2.4.2.12.6 Kilesasaṃkiliṭṭhaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Kilesagocchaka
Kilesasaṃkiliṭṭhaduka
1575.
2286Katame dhammā kilesā ceva saṃkiliṭṭhā ca? Teva kilesā kilesā ceva saṃkiliṭṭhā ca.
1576.
2287Katame dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā? Ṭhapetvā kilese avasesaṃ akusalaṃ—  ime dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā. Asaṃkiliṭṭhā dhammā na vattabbā—  kilesā ceva saṃkiliṭṭhā cātipi, saṃkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesātipi.
