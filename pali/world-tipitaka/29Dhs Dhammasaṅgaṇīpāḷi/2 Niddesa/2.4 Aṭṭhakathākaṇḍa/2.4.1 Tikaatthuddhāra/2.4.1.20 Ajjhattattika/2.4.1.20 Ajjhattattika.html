2.4.1.20 Ajjhattattika
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Tikaatthuddhāra
Ajjhattattika
1435.
2141Anindriyabaddharūpañca nibbānañca ṭhapetvā, sabbe dhammā siyā ajjhattā, siyā bahiddhā, siyā ajjhattabahiddhā. Anindriyabaddharūpañca nibbānañca bahiddhā.
