2.4.1.22 Sanidassanattika
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Tikaatthuddhāra
Sanidassanattika
1438.
2144Katame dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighā? Rūpāyatanaṃ—  ime dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighā.
1439.
2145Katame dhammā anidassanasappaṭighā? Cakkhāyatanaṃ…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ—  ime dhammā anidassanasappaṭighā.
1440.
2146Katame dhammā anidassanaappaṭighā? Catūsu bhūmīsu kusalaṃ, akusalaṃ, catūsu bhūmīsu vipāko, tīsu bhūmīsu kiriyābyākataṃ, yañca rūpaṃ anidassanaṃ appaṭighaṃ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṃ, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā anidassanaappaṭighā.
2147Tikaṃ.
