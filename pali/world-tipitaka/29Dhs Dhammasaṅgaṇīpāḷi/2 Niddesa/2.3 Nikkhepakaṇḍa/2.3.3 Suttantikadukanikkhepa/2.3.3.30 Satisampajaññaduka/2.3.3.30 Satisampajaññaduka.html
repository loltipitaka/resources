2.3.3.30 Satisampajaññaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Satisampajaññaduka
1358.
2063Tattha katamā sati? Yā sati anussati paṭissati sati saraṇatā dhāraṇatā apilāpanatā asammussanatā sati satindriyaṃ satibalaṃ sammāsati—  ayaṃ vuccati sati.
1359.
2064Tattha katamaṃ sampajaññaṃ? Yā paññā pajānanā…pe…  amoho dhammavicayo sammādiṭṭhi—  idaṃ vuccati sampajaññaṃ.
