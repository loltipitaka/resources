2.3.3.7 Niruttiduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Niruttiduka
1314.
2017Katame dhammā nirutti? Yā tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ saṅkhā samaññā paññatti vohāro nāmaṃ nāmakammaṃ nāmadheyyaṃ nirutti byañjanaṃ abhilāpo—  ime dhammā nirutti.
2018Sabbeva dhammā niruttipathā.
