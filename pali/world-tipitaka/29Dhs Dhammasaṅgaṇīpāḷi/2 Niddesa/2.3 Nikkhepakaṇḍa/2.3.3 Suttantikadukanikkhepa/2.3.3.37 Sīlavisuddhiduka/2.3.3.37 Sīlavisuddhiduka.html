2.3.3.37 Sīlavisuddhiduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Sīlavisuddhiduka
1372.
2077Tattha katamā sīlavisuddhi? Yo kāyiko avītikkamo, vācasiko avītikkamo, kāyikavācasiko avītikkamo—  ayaṃ vuccati sīlavisuddhi. Sabbopi sīlasaṃvaro sīlavisuddhi.
1373.
2078Tattha katamā diṭṭhivisuddhi? Kammassakatañāṇaṃ saccānulomikañāṇaṃ maggasamaṅgissa ñāṇaṃ phalasamaṅgissa ñāṇaṃ.
