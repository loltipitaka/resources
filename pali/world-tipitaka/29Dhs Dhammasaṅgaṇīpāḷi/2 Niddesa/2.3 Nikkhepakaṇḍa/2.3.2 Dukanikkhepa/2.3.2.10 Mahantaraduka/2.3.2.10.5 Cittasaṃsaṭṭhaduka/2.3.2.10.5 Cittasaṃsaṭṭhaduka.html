2.3.2.10.5 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaduka
1199.
1895Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhā.
1200.
1896Katame dhammā cittavisaṃsaṭṭhā? Sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā cittavisaṃsaṭṭhā. Cittaṃ na vattabbaṃ—  cittena saṃsaṭṭhantipi, cittena visaṃsaṭṭhantipi.
