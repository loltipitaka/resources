2.3.2.10.10 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka
1209.
1905Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.
1210.
1906Katame dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Cittañca, sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.
