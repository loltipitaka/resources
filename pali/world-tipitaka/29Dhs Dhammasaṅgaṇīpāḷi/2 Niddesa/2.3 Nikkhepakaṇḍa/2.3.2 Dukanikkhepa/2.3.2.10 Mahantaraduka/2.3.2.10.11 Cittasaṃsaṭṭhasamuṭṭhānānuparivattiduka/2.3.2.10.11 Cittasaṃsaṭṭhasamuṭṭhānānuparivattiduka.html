2.3.2.10.11 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka
1211.
1907Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.
1212.
1908Katame dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Cittañca, sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.
