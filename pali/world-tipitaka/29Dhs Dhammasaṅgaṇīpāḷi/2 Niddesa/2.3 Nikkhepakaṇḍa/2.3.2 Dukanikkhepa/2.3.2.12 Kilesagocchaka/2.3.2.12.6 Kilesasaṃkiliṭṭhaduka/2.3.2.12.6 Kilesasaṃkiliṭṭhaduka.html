2.3.2.12.6 Kilesasaṃkiliṭṭhaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Kilesagocchaka
Kilesasaṃkiliṭṭhaduka
1255.
1954Katame dhammā kilesā ceva saṃkiliṭṭhā ca? Teva kilesā kilesā ceva saṃkiliṭṭhā ca.
1256.
1955Katame dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā? Tehi dhammehi ye dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhā, te dhamme ṭhapetvā vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā.
