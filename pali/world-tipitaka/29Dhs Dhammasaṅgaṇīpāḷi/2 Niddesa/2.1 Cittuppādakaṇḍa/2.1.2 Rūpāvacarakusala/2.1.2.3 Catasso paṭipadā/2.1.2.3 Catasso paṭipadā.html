176.
592Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati dukkhapaṭipadaṃ dandhābhiññaṃ pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.

596Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vitakkavicārānaṃ vūpasamā…pe…  dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  tatiyaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  catutthaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  pañcamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati dukkhapaṭipadaṃ dandhābhiññaṃ pathavīkasiṇaṃ…pe…  dukkhapaṭipadaṃ khippābhiññaṃ pathavīkasiṇaṃ…pe…  sukhapaṭipadaṃ dandhābhiññaṃ pathavīkasiṇaṃ…pe…  sukhapaṭipadaṃ khippābhiññaṃ pathavīkasiṇaṃ—  tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
