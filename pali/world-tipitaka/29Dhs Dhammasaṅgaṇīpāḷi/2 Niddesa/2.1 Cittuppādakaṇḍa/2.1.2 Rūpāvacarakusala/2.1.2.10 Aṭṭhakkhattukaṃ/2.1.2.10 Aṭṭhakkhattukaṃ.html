214.
643Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti ajjhattaṃ arūpasaññī bahiddhā rūpāni passati parittāni, tāni abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmīti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati dukkhapaṭipadaṃ dandhābhiññaṃ parittaṃ parittārammaṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
