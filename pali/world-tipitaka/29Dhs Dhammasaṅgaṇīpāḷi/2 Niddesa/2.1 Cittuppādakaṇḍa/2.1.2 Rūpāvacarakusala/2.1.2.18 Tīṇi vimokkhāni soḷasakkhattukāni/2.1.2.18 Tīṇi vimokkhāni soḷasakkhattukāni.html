2.1.2.18 Tīṇi vimokkhāni soḷasakkhattukāni
Niddesa
Cittuppādakaṇḍa
Rūpāvacarakusala

691Tīṇi vimokkhāni soḷasakkhattukāni

248.
692Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti rūpī rūpāni passati vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
249.
693Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti ajjhattaṃ arūpasaññī bahiddhā rūpāni passati vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
250.
694Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti subhanti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
695Imānipi tīṇi vimokkhāni soḷasakkhattukāni.
