2.2.3.10 Dasaka
Niddesa
Rūpakaṇḍa
Rūpavibhatti
Dasaka
978.
1640Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ cakkhundriyaṃ? Yaṃ cakkhu catunnaṃ mahābhūtānaṃ upādāya pasādo…pe…  suñño gāmopeso—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ cakkhundriyaṃ.
979.
1641Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ sotindriyaṃ…pe…  ghānindriyaṃ…pe…  jivhindriyaṃ…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ…pe…  itthindriyaṃ…pe…  purisindriyaṃ…pe…  jīvitindriyaṃ? Yo tesaṃ rūpīnaṃ dhammānaṃ āyu ṭhiti yapanā yāpanā iriyanā vattanā pālanā jīvitaṃ jīvitindriyaṃ—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ jīvitindriyaṃ.
980.
1642Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ sappaṭighaṃ? Rūpāyatanaṃ…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ sappaṭighaṃ.
981.
1643Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ appaṭighaṃ? Kāyaviññatti…pe…  kabaḷīkāro āhāro—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ appaṭighaṃ.
1644Evaṃ dasavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.
Dasakaṃ.
