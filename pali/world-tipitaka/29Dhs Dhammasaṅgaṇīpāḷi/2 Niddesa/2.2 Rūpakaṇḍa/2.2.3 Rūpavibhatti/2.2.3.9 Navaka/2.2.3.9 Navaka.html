2.2.3.9 Navaka
Niddesa
Rūpakaṇḍa
Rūpavibhatti
Navaka
975.
1636Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ cakkhundriyaṃ? Yaṃ cakkhu catunnaṃ mahābhūtānaṃ upādāya pasādo…pe…  suñño gāmopeso—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ cakkhundriyaṃ.
976.
1637Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ sotindriyaṃ…pe…  ghānindriyaṃ…pe…  jivhindriyaṃ…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ…pe…  itthindriyaṃ…pe…  purisindriyaṃ…pe…  jīvitindriyaṃ? Yo tesaṃ rūpīnaṃ dhammānaṃ āyu ṭhiti yapanā yāpanā iriyanā vattanā pālanā jīvitaṃ jīvitindriyaṃ—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ jīvitindriyaṃ.
977.
1638Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ? Rūpāyatanaṃ…pe…  kabaḷīkāro āhāro—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ.
1639Evaṃ navavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.
Navakaṃ.
