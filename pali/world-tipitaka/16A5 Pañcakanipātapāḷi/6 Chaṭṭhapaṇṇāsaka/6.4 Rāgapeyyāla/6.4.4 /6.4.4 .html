6.4.4
Chaṭṭhapaṇṇāsaka
Rāgapeyyāla
306.
1409“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ—  rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti. (4)
