4.1.10 Duppaṭivinodayasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Saddhammavagga
Duppaṭivinodayasutta
160.
927“Pañcime, bhikkhave, uppannā duppaṭivinodayā. Katame pañca? Uppanno rāgo duppaṭivinodayo, uppanno doso duppaṭivinodayo, uppanno moho duppaṭivinodayo, uppannaṃ paṭibhānaṃ duppaṭivinodayaṃ, uppannaṃ gamikacittaṃ duppaṭivinodayaṃ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca uppannā duppaṭivinodayā”ti.
928Dasamaṃ.
929Saddhammavaggo paṭhamo.
930Tassuddānaṃ
931
Tayo sammattaniyāmā,
tayo saddhammasammosā;
Dukkathā ceva sārajjaṃ,
udāyidubbinodayāti.
