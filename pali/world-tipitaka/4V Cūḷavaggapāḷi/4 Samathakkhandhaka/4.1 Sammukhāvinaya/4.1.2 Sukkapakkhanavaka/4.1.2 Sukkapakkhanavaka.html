4.1.2 Sukkapakkhanavaka
Samathakkhandhaka
Sammukhāvinaya
Sukkapakkhanavaka
188.
797Dhammavādī puggalo adhammavādiṃ puggalaṃ saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhāhi, imaṃ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (1)
798Dhammavādī puggalo adhammavādī sambahule saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhatha, imaṃ rocethā’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (2)
799Dhammavādī puggalo adhammavādiṃ saṃghaṃ saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhāhi, imaṃ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (3)
800Dhammavādī sambahulā adhammavādiṃ puggalaṃ saññāpenti nijjhāpenti pekkhenti anupekkhenti dassenti anudassenti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhāhi, imaṃ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (4)
801Dhammavādī sambahulā adhammavādī sambahule saññāpenti nijjhāpenti pekkhenti anupekkhenti dassenti anudassenti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhatha, imaṃ rocethā’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (5)
802Dhammavādī sambahulā adhammavādiṃ saṃghaṃ saññāpenti nijjhāpenti pekkhenti anupekkhenti dassenti anudassenti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhāhi, imaṃ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (6)
803Dhammavādī saṃgho adhammavādiṃ puggalaṃ saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhāhi, imaṃ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (7)
804Dhammavādī saṃgho adhammavādī sambahule saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhatha, imaṃ rocethā’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (8)
805Dhammavādī saṃgho adhammavādiṃ saṃghaṃ saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti—  ‘ayaṃ dhammo, ayaṃ vinayo, idaṃ satthusāsanaṃ, imaṃ gaṇhāhi, imaṃ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayenā”ti. (9)
806Sukkapakkhanavakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
