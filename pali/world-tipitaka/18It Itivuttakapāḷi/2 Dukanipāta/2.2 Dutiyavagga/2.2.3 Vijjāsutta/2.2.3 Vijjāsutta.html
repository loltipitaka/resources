2.2.3 Vijjāsutta
Dukanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Vijjāsutta
40.
251Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
252“Avijjā, bhikkhave, pubbaṅgamā akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ samāpattiyā anvadeva ahirikaṃ anottappaṃ; vijjā ca kho, bhikkhave, pubbaṅgamā kusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ samāpattiyā anvadeva hirottappan”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
253
“Yā kācimā duggatiyo,
asmiṃ loke paramhi ca;
Avijjāmūlikā sabbā,
icchālobhasamussayā.
254
Yato ca hoti pāpiccho,
ahirīko anādaro;
Tato pāpaṃ pasavati,
apāyaṃ tena gacchati.
255
Tasmā chandañca lobhañca,
avijjañca virājayaṃ;
Vijjaṃ uppādayaṃ bhikkhu,
sabbā duggatiyo jahe”ti.
256Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
257Tatiyaṃ.
258Paṭhamabhāṇavāro.
