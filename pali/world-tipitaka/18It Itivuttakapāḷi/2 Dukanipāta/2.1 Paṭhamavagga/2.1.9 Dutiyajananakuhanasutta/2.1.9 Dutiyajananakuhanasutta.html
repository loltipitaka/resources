2.1.9 Dutiyajananakuhanasutta
Dukanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Dutiyajananakuhanasutta
36.
220Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
221“Nayidaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati janakuhanatthaṃ, na janalapanatthaṃ, na lābhasakkārasilokānisaṃsatthaṃ, na ‘iti maṃ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati abhiññatthañceva pariññatthañcā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
222
“Abhiññatthaṃ pariññatthaṃ,
brahmacariyaṃ anītihaṃ;
Adesayi so bhagavā,
nibbānogadhagāminaṃ.
223
Esa maggo mahattehi,
anuyāto mahesibhi;
Ye ye taṃ paṭipajjanti,
yathā buddhena desitaṃ;
Dukkhassantaṃ karissanti,
satthusāsanakārino”ti.
224Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
225Navamaṃ.
