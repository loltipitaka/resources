2.1.8 Paṭhamajananakuhanasutta
Dukanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Paṭhamajananakuhanasutta
35.
214Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
215“Nayidaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati janakuhanatthaṃ, na janalapanatthaṃ, na lābhasakkārasilokānisaṃsatthaṃ, na ‘iti maṃ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati saṃvaratthañceva pahānatthañcā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
216
“Saṃvaratthaṃ pahānatthaṃ,
brahmacariyaṃ anītihaṃ;
Adesayi so bhagavā,
nibbānogadhagāminaṃ.
217
Esa maggo mahattehi,
anuyāto mahesibhi;
Ye ye taṃ paṭipajjanti,
yathā buddhena desitaṃ;
Dukkhassantaṃ karissanti,
satthusāsanakārino”ti.
218Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
219Aṭṭhamaṃ.
