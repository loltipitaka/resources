3.2.3 Indriyasutta
Tikanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Indriyasutta
62.
399Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
400“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ, aññindriyaṃ, aññātāvindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
401
“Sekhassa sikkhamānassa,
ujumaggānusārino;
Khayasmiṃ paṭhamaṃ ñāṇaṃ,
tato aññā anantarā.
402
Tato aññā vimuttassa,
ñāṇaṃ ve hoti tādino;
Akuppā me vimuttīti,
bhavasaṃyojanakkhayā.
403
Sa ve indriyasampanno,
santo santipade rato;
Dhāreti antimaṃ dehaṃ,
jetvā māraṃ savāhinin”ti.
404Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
405Tatiyaṃ.
