1.3.1.4 Upādinnattika
Khandhavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Tika
Upādinnattika
503Rūpakkhandho siyā upādinnupādāniyo, siyā anupādinnupādāniyo. Cattāro khandhā siyā upādinnupādāniyā, siyā anupādinnupādāniyā, siyā anupādinnaanupādāniyā.
