1.3.1.21 Ajjhattārammaṇattika
Khandhavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Tika
Ajjhattārammaṇattika
520Rūpakkhandho anārammaṇo. Cattāro khandhā siyā ajjhattārammaṇā, siyā bahiddhārammaṇā, siyā ajjhattabahiddhārammaṇā, siyā na vattabbā—  “ajjhattārammaṇā”tipi, “bahiddhārammaṇā”tipi, “ajjhattabahiddhārammaṇā”tipi.
