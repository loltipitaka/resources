1.3.1.5 Saṃkiliṭṭhattika
Khandhavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Tika
Saṃkiliṭṭhattika
504Rūpakkhandho asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko. Cattāro khandhā siyā saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikā, siyā asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikā, siyā asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikā.
