15.1.3 Hetuvāra
Paṭisambhidāvibhaṅga
Suttantabhājanīya
Hetuvāra
720.
2120Catasso paṭisambhidā—  atthapaṭisambhidā, dhammapaṭisambhidā niruttipaṭisambhidā, paṭibhānapaṭisambhidā. Hetumhi ñāṇaṃ dhammapaṭisambhidā, hetuphale ñāṇaṃ atthapaṭisambhidā, tatra dhammaniruttābhilāpe ñāṇaṃ niruttipaṭisambhidā, ñāṇesu ñāṇaṃ paṭibhānapaṭisambhidā. Ayaṃ hetuvāro.
