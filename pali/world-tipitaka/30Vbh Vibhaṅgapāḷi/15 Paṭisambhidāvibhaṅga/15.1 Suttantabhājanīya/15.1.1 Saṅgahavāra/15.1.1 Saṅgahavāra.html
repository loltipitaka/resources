15.1.1 Saṅgahavāra
Paṭisambhidāvibhaṅga
Suttantabhājanīya
Saṅgahavāra
718.
2118Catasso paṭisambhidā—  atthapaṭisambhidā, dhammapaṭisambhidā, niruttipaṭisambhidā, paṭibhānapaṭisambhidā. Atthe ñāṇaṃ atthapaṭisambhidā, dhamme ñāṇaṃ dhammapaṭisambhidā, tatra dhammaniruttābhilāpe ñāṇaṃ niruttipaṭisambhidā, ñāṇesu ñāṇaṃ paṭibhānapaṭisambhidā. Ayaṃ saṅgahavāro.
