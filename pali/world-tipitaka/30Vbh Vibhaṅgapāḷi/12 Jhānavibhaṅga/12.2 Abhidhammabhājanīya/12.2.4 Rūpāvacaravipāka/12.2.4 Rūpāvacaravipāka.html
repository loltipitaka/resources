12.2.4 Rūpāvacaravipāka
Jhānavibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
Rūpāvacaravipāka
630.
1893Cattāri jhānāni—  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ, dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ, tatiyaṃ jhānaṃ, catutthaṃ jhānaṃ.
631.
1894Tattha katamaṃ paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ? Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti. Ime dhammā kusalā. Tasseva rūpāvacarassa kusalassa kammassa katattā upacitattā vipākaṃ vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye pañcaṅgikaṃ jhānaṃ hoti—  vitakko, vicāro, pīti, sukhaṃ, cittassekaggatā. Idaṃ vuccati “paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ”. Avasesā dhammā jhānasampayuttā.
1895Tattha katamaṃ dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ? Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vitakkavicārānaṃ vūpasamā…pe…  dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti. Ime dhammā kusalā. Tasseva rūpāvacarassa kusalassa kammassa katattā upacitattā vipākaṃ vitakkavicārānaṃ vūpasamā…pe…  dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  tatiyaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  catutthaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ…pe…  pañcamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye duvaṅgikaṃ jhānaṃ hoti—  upekkhā, cittassekaggatā. Idaṃ vuccati “pañcamaṃ jhānaṃ”. Avasesā dhammā jhānasampayuttā…pe… .
