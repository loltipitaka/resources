13.2.1 Kusala
Appamaññāvibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
Kusala
683.
1990Catasso appamaññāyo—  mettā, karuṇā, muditā, upekkhā.
684.
1991Tattha katamā mettā? Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati mettāsahagataṃ, yā tasmiṃ samaye metti mettāyanā mettāyitattaṃ mettācetovimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati “mettā”. Avasesā dhammā mettāya sampayuttā. (1:1)
1992Tattha katamā mettā? Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vitakkavicārānaṃ vūpasamā…pe…  dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati mettāsahagataṃ, yā tasmiṃ samaye metti mettāyanā mettāyitattaṃ mettācetovimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati “mettā”. Avasesā dhammā mettāya sampayuttā. (2:2)
