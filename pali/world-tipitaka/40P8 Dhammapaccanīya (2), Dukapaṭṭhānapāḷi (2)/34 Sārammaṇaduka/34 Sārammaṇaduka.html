167Nasārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo ca naanārammaṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

169Nasārammaṇañca naanārammaṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasārammaṇañca naanārammaṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasārammaṇañca naanārammaṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo ca naanārammaṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
