5 Kusalattika, Cūḷantaraduka
Kusalattika, Cūḷantaraduka
10.
31Nakusalo nasappaccayo dhammo kusalassa sappaccayassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo… . Ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā upanissaye cha. (Saṅkhataṃ sappaccayasadisaṃ.)
11.
32Nakusalaṃ nasanidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato sanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
33Nakusalo naanidassano dhammo kusalassa anidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Nava pañhā kātabbā.) Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā tīṇi, upanissaye purejāte atthiyā avigate nava.
12.
34Nakusalaṃ nasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato sappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
35Nakusalaṃ naappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato appaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
13.
36Nakusalaṃ narūpiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato rūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
37Nakusalaṃ naarūpiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato arūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
14.
38Nakusalaṃ nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.
39Nakusalaṃ nalokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṃ nalokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā abyākato lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Cha pañhā kātabbā.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
15.
40Nakusalaṃ nakenaci viññeyyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo kenaci viññeyyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā aṭṭhārasa, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate aṭṭhārasa.
41Nakusalaṃ nakenaci naviññeyyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo kenaci viññeyyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā aṭṭhārasa, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate aṭṭhārasa.
