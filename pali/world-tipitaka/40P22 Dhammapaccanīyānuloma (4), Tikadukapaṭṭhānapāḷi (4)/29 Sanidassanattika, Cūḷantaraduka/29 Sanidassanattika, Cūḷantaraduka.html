29 Sanidassanattika, Cūḷantaraduka
Sanidassanattika, Cūḷantaraduka
63.
149Nasanidassanasappaṭigho nasappaccayo dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa sappaccayassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā upanissaye tīṇi.
64.
150Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nasanidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho sanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.
151Naanidassanaappaṭigho nanasanidassano dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa nasanidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā upanissaye purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.
65.
152Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho sappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.
153Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho appaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
66.
154Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ narūpiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho rūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  avigate ekavīsa.
155Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naarūpiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho arūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
67.
156Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  satta.
157Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nalokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
158Naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanasappaṭigho lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  satta. Hetuyā ekavīsa.
159Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nalokiyañca naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nalokiyañca dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  satta. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa…pe…  avigate ekavīsa.
160Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nalokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
68.
161Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nakenaci viññeyyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho kenaci viññeyyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañcatiṃsa, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe pannarasa…pe…  avigate pañcatiṃsa.
162Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nanakenaci viññeyyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho nakenaci viññeyyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañcatiṃsa, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe pannarasa…pe…  avigate pañcatiṃsa.
