982Āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  āsave paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (1)
983Noāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  noāsave khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe… . (Yāva asaññasattā.) (1)
984Āsavañca noāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  āsave ca sampayuttake ca khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
