Hetu
47.
1088Sāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sāsavaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ kaṭattārūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ. (1)
1089Anāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  anāsavaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1090Anāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  anāsave khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (2)
1091Anāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo ca anāsavo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  anāsavaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
1092Sāsavañca anāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  anāsave khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (1)
1093(Yathā cūḷantaraduke lokiyadukaṃ, evaṃ kātabbaṃ, ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)
1094Sāsavadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
