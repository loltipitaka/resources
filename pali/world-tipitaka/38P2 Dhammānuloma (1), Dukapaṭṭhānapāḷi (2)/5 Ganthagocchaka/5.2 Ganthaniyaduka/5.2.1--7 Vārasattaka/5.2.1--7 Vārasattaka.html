Ganthaniyaduka
Vārasattaka
44.
1716Ganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  ganthaniyaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1717(Yathā cūḷantaraduke lokiyadukaṃ, evaṃ vibhajitabbaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)
1718Ganthaniyadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
