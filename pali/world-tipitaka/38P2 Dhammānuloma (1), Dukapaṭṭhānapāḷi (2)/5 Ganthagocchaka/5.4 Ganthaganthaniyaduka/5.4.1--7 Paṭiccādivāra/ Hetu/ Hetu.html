Ganthagocchaka
Ganthaganthaniyaduka
Paṭiccādivāra
Hetu
103.
1842Ganthañceva ganthaniyañca dhammaṃ paṭicca gantho ceva ganthaniyo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sīlabbataparāmāsaṃ kāyaganthaṃ paṭicca abhijjhākāyagantho, abhijjhākāyaganthaṃ paṭicca sīlabbataparāmāso kāyagantho, idaṃsaccābhinivesakāyaganthaṃ paṭicca abhijjhākāyagantho, abhijjhākāyaganthaṃ paṭicca idaṃsaccābhinivesakāyagantho. (1)
1843Ganthañceva ganthaniyañca dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthaniyo ceva no ca gantho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  ganthe paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ. (2)
1844Ganthañceva ganthaniyañca dhammaṃ paṭicca gantho ceva ganthaniyo ca ganthaniyo ceva no ca gantho ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (1)
