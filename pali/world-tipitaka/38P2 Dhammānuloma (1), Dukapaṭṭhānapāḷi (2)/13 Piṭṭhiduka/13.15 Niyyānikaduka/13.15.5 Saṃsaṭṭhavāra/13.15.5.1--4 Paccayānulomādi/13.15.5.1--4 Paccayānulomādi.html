Paccayānulomādi
312.
4737Niyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho niyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  niyyānikaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
4738Aniyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho aniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  aniyyānikaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
4739Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe dve (sabbattha dve), vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve. (Anulomaṃ.)
4740Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā dve, napurejāte dve, napacchājāte dve, naāsevane ekaṃ, nakamme dve, navipāke dve, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte dve. (Paccanīyaṃ.)
