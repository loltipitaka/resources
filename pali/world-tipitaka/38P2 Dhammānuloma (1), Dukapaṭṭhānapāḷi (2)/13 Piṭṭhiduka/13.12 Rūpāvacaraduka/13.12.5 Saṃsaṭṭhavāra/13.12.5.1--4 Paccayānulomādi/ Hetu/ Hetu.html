Hetu
244.
4609Rūpāvacaraṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho rūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  rūpāvacaraṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
4610Narūpāvacaraṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho narūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  narūpāvacaraṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
4611Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe dve (sabbattha dve), avigate dve.
4612Anulomaṃ.
4613Narūpāvacaraṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho narūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
4614Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā dve, napurejāte dve, napacchājāte dve, naāsevane dve, nakamme dve, navipāke dve, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte ekaṃ.
4615Paccanīyaṃ.
