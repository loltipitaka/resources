4711Pariyāpannaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca pariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  pariyāpannaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (Yathā cūḷantaraduke lokiyadukaṃ. Evaṃ imampi dukaṃ kātabbaṃ, ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)
