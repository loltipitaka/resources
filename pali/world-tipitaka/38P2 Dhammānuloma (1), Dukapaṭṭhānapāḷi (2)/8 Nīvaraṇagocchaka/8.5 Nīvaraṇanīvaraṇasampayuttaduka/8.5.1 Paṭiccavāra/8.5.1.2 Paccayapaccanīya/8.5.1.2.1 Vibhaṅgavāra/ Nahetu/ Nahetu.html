96.
2100Nīvaraṇañceva nīvaraṇasampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nīvaraṇo ceva nīvaraṇasampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchānīvaraṇaṃ paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṃ, uddhaccanīvaraṇaṃ paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṃ. (1)
2101Nīvaraṇasampayuttañceva no ca nīvaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nīvaraṇo ceva nīvaraṇasampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṃ. (1)
2102Nīvaraṇañceva nīvaraṇasampayuttañca nīvaraṇasampayuttañceva no ca nīvaraṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nīvaraṇo ceva nīvaraṇasampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchānīvaraṇañca sampayuttake ca khandhe paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṃ, uddhaccanīvaraṇañca sampayuttake ca khandhe paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
