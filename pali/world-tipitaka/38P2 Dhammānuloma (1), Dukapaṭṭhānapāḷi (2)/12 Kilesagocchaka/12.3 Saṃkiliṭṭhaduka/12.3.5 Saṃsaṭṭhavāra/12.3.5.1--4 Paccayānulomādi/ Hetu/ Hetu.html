49.
3942Saṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  saṃkiliṭṭhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
3943Asaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  asaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
3944Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe dve, adhipatiyā dve (sabbattha dve), vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
3945Anulomaṃ.
50.
3946Saṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe saṃsaṭṭho vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
3947Asaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukaṃ asaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  ahetukapaṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
3948Nahetuyā dve, naadhipatiyā dve, napurejāte dve, napacchājāte dve, naāsevane dve, nakamme dve, navipāke dve, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte dve.
3949Paccanīyaṃ.
