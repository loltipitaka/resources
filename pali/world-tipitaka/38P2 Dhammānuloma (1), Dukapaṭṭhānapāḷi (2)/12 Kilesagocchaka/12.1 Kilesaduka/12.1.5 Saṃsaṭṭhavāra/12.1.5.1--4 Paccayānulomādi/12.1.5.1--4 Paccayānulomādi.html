12.1.5.1--4 Paccayānulomādi
Kilesagocchaka
Kilesaduka
Saṃsaṭṭhavāra
Paccayānulomādi
15.
3849Kilesaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho kileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lobhaṃ saṃsaṭṭho moho diṭṭhi thinaṃ uddhaccaṃ ahirikaṃ anottappaṃ. (Cakkaṃ. Evaṃ nava pañhā kātabbā.)
3850Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.
3851Anulomaṃ.
3852Kilesaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho kileso dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā (evaṃ nahetupañhā cattāri kātabbā.)
3853Nahetuyā cattāri, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte nava.
3854Paccanīyaṃ.
