84.
4027Kilesañceva saṃkiliṭṭhañca dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso ceva saṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchaṃ paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato moho, uddhaccaṃ paṭicca uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
4028Saṃkiliṭṭhañceva no ca kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso ceva saṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
4029Kilesañceva saṃkiliṭṭhañca saṃkiliṭṭhañceva no ca kilesañca dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso ceva saṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca vicikicchañca uddhaccañca paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
