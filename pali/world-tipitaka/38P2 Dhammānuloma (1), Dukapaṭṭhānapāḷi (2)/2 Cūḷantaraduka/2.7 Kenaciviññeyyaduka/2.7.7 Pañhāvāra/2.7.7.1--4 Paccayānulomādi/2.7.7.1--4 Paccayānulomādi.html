181.
963Kenaci viññeyyo dhammo kenaci viññeyyassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  kenaci viññeyyā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
964Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  avigate nava.
965Anulomaṃ.
966Nahetuyā nava…pe…  novigate nava. (Evaṃ cattāripi gaṇanā paripuṇṇā.)
967Paccanīyaṃ.
Kenaciviññeyyadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
968Cūḷantaradukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
