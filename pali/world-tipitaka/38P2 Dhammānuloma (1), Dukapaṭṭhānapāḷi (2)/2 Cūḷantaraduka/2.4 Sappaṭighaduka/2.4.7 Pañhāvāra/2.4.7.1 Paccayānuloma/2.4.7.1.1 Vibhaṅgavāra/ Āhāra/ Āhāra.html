720Appaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo—  appaṭighā āhārā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ appaṭighānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . Kabaḷīkāro āhāro—  imassa appaṭighassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (Avasesā dvepi pañhā kātabbā, paṭisandhi kabaḷīkāro āhāro dvīsupi kātabbo agge.) (3)
