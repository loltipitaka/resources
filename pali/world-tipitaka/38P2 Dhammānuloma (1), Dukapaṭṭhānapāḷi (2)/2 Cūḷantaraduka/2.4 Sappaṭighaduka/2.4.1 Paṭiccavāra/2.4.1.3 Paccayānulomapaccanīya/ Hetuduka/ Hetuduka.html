683Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe nava, naadhipatiyā nava, naanantare nava, nasamanantare nava, naaññamaññe nava, naupanissaye nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme ekaṃ, navipāke nava, nasampayutte nava, navippayutte ekaṃ, nonatthiyā nava, novigate nava.
