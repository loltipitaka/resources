82.
224Sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā ahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paccayā vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
225Ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā ahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  (yāva asaññasattā), cakkhāyatanaṃ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṃ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ paccayā kāyaviññāṇaṃ, vatthuṃ paccayā ahetukā khandhā moho ca. (1)
226Sahetukañca ahetukañca dhammaṃ paccayā ahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca vatthuñca paccayā vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
