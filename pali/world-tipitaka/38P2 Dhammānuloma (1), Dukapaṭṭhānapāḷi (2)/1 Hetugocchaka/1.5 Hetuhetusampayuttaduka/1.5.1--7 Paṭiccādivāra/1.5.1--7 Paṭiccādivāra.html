438Hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ceva hetusampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  alobhaṃ paṭicca adoso amoho (cakkaṃ). Lobhaṃ paṭicca moho (cakkaṃ); paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (Yathā hetusahetukadukaṃ evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ, ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)
439Hetuhetusampayuttadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
