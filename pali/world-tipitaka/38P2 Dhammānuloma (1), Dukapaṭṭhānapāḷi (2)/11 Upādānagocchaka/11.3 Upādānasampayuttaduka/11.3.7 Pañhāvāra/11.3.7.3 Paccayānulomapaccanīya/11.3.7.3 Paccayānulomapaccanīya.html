11.3.7.3 Paccayānulomapaccanīya
Upādānagocchaka
Upādānasampayuttaduka
Pañhāvāra
Paccayānulomapaccanīya
93.
3755Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe nava, naadhipatiyā nava, naanantare nava, nasamanantare nava, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye nava (sabbattha nava), nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte cha, nonatthiyā nava, novigate nava.
