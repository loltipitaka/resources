301.
3057Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo—  cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāne khandhe ārabbha cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā khandhā uppajjanti. (Mūlaṃ kātabbaṃ.) Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāne khandhe ārabbha cittaṃ uppajjati. (Mūlaṃ kātabbaṃ.) Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāne khandhe ārabbha cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā uppajjanti. (3)
3058Nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo—  ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā maggaṃ paccavekkhanti…pe…  nibbānaṃ paccavekkhanti. Nibbānaṃ gotrabhussa…pe… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Yathā cittasahabhūduke ārammaṇaṃ evaṃ kātabbaṃ, ninnānākaraṇaṃ. Navapi pañhā.)
