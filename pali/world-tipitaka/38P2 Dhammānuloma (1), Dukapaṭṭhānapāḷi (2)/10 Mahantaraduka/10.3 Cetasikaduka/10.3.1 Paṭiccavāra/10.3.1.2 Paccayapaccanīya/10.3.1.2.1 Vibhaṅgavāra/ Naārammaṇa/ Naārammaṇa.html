2571Cetasikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca acetasiko dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  cetasike khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
2572Acetasikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca acetasiko dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  cittaṃ paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (Yāva asaññasattā.) (1)
2573Cetasikañca acetasikañca dhammaṃ paṭicca acetasiko dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  cetasike khandhe ca cittañca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ, cetasike khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (Paṭisandhikkhaṇe dvepi kātabbā, saṃkhittaṃ.)
