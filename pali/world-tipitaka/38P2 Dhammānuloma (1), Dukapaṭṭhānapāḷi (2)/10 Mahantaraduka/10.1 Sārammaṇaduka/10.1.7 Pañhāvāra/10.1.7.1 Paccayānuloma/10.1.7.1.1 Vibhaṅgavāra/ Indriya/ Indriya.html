38.
2396Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
2397Anārammaṇo dhammo anārammaṇassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  rūpajīvitindriyaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2398Anārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2399Sārammaṇo ca anārammaṇo ca dhammā sārammaṇassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  cakkhundriyañca cakkhuviññāṇañca cakkhuviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo…pe…  kāyindriyañca kāyaviññāṇañca kāyaviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
