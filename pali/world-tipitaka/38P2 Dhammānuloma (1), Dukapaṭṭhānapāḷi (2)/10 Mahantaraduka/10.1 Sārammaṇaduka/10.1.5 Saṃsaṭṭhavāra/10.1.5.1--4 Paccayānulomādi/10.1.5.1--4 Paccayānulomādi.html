10.1.5.1--4 Paccayānulomādi
Mahantaraduka
Sārammaṇaduka
Saṃsaṭṭhavāra
Paccayānulomādi
23.
2368Sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sārammaṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… .
24.
2369Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ (sabbattha ekaṃ), avigate ekaṃ.
2370Anulomaṃ.
25.
2371Sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukaṃ sārammaṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe saṃsaṭṭho vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
26.
2372Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā ekaṃ, napurejāte ekaṃ, napacchājāte ekaṃ, naāsevane ekaṃ, nakamme ekaṃ, navipāke ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte ekaṃ.
2373Paccanīyaṃ.
