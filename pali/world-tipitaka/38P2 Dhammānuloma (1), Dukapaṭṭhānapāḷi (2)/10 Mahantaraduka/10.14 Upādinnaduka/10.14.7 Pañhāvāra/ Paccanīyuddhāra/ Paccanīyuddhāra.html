Mahantaraduka
Upādinnaduka
Pañhāvāra
Paccanīyuddhāra
467.
3513Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3514Upādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3515Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa ca anupādinnassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (3)
468.
3516Anupādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3517Anupādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3518Anupādinno dhammo upādinnassa ca anupādinnassa ca dhammassa pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo. (3)
3519Upādinno ca anupādinno ca dhammā upādinnassa dhammassa purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (1)
3520Upādinno ca anupādinno ca dhammā anupādinnassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ. (2)
3521Upādinno ca anupādinno ca dhammā upādinnassa ca anupādinnassa ca dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (3)
