2683Cittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca cittasampayutto dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukaṃ cittasampayuttaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca dve khandhā, dve khandhe…pe…  ahetukapaṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
2684(Evaṃ navapi pañhā kātabbā. Ahetukanti sabbattha niyāmetabbaṃ, ekaṃyeva mohaṃ mūlapade.)
