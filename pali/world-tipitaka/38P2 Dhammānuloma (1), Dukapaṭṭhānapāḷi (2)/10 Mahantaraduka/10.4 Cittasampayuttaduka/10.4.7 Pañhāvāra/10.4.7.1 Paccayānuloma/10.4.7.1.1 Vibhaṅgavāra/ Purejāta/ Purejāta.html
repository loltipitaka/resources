Purejāta
170.
2728Cittavippayutto dhammo cittasampayuttassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ—  cakkhuṃ…pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato…pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti. Rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ…pe… . Vatthupurejātaṃ—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ…pe…  vatthu cittasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
