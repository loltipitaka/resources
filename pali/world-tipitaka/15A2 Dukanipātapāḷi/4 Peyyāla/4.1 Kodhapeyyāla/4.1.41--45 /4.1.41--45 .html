4.1.41--45
Peyyāla
Kodhapeyyāla
189.
284“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato idhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapajjati. Katamehi dvīhi? Kodhena ca upanāhena ca…  makkhena ca paḷāsena ca…  issāya ca macchariyena ca…  māyāya ca sāṭheyyena ca…  ahirikena ca anottappena ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato idhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapajjati”.
