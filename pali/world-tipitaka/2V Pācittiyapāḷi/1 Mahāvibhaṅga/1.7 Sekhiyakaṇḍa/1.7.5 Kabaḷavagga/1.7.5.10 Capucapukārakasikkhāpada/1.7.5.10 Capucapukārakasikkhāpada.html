1.7.5.10 Capucapukārakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Kabaḷavagga
Capucapukārakasikkhāpada
626.
1934Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjanti…pe… .
1935“Na capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (50:195)
1936Na capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1937Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1938Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
Kabaḷavaggo pañcamo.
