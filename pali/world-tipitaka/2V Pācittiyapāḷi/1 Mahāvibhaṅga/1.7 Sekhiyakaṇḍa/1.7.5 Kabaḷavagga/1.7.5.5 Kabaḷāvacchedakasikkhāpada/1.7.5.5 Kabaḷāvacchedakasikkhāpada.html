1.7.5.5 Kabaḷāvacchedakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Kabaḷavagga
Kabaḷāvacchedakasikkhāpada
621.
1909Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū kabaḷāvacchedakaṃ bhuñjanti…pe… .
1910“Na kabaḷāvacchedakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (45:190)
1911Na kabaḷāvacchedakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca kabaḷāvacchedakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1912Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, khajjake phalāphale, uttaribhaṅge, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1913Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
