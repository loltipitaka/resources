1.7.5.3 Sakabaḷasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Kabaḷavagga
Sakabaḷasikkhāpada
619.
1899Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sakabaḷena mukhena byāharanti…pe… .
1900“Na sakabaḷena mukhena byāharissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (43:188)
1901Na sakabaḷena mukhena byāharitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sakabaḷena mukhena byāharati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1902Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1903Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
