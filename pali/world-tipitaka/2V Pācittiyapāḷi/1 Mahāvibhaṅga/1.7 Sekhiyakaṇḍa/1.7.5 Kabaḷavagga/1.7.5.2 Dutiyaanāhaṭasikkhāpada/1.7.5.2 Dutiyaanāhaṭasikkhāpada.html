1.7.5.2 Dutiyaanāhaṭasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Kabaḷavagga
Dutiyaanāhaṭasikkhāpada
618.
1894Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhuñjamānā sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipanti…pe… .
1895“Na bhuñjamāno sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (42:187)
1896Na bhuñjamānena sabbo hattho mukhe pakkhipitabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca bhuñjamāno sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1897Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1898Dutiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
