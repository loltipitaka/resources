1.7.3.1 Khambhakatasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
Khambhakatasikkhāpada
596.
1785Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū khambhakatā antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .
1786“Na khambhakato antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (21:166)
1787Na khambhakatena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ekato vā ubhato vā khambhaṃ katvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1788Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
1789Paṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
