1.7.7.11 Pacchatogamanasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Pādukavagga
Pacchatogamanasikkhāpada
649.
2062Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pacchato gacchantā purato gacchantassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .
2063“Na pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (71:216)
2064Na pacchato gacchantena purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
2065Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
2066Ekādasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
