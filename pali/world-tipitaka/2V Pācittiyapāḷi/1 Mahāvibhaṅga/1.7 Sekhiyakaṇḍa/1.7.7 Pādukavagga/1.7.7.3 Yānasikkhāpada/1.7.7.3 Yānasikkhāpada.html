1.7.7.3 Yānasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Pādukavagga
Yānasikkhāpada
640.
2012Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū yānagatassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .
2013“Na yānagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (63:208)
2014Yānaṃ nāma vayhaṃ ratho sakaṭaṃ sandamānikā sivikā pāṭaṅkī.
2015Na yānagatassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca yānagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
2016Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
2017Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
