1.7.7.5 Pallatthikasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Pādukavagga
Pallatthikasikkhāpada
642.
2023Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pallatthikāya nisinnassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .
2024“Na pallatthikāya nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (65:210)
2025Na pallatthikāya nisinnassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthapallatthikāya vā dussapallatthikāya vā nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
2026Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
2027Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
