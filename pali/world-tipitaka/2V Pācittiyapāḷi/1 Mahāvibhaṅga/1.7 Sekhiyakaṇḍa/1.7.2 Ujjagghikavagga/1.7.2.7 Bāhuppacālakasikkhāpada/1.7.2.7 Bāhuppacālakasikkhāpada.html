1.7.2.7 Bāhuppacālakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Ujjagghikavagga
Bāhuppacālakasikkhāpada
592.
1765Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bāhuppacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchanti bāhuṃ olambentā…pe… .
1766“Na bāhuppacālakaṃ antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (17:162)
1767Na bāhuppacālakaṃ antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Bāhuṃ paggahetvā gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca bāhuppacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchati bāhuṃ olambento, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1768Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
1769Sattamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
