1.7.6.9 Satthapāṇisikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Surusuruvagga
Satthapāṇisikkhāpada
636.
1990Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū satthapāṇissa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .
1991“Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (59:204)
1992Satthaṃ nāma ekatodhāraṃ ubhatodhāraṃ paharaṇaṃ.
1993Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1994Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
1995Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
