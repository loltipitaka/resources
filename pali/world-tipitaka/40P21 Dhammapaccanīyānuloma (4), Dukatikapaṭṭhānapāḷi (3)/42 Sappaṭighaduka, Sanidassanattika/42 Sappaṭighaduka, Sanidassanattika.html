42 Sappaṭighaduka, Sanidassanattika
Sappaṭighaduka, Sanidassanattika
90.
227Nasappaṭighaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sappaṭigho sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
228Naappaṭighaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sappaṭigho sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
229Nasappaṭighaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighañca naappaṭighaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighañca dhammaṃ paṭicca sappaṭigho sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Sabbattha tīṇi.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
230Nasappaṭighaṃ naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sappaṭigho anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
231Naappaṭighaṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca appaṭigho anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
