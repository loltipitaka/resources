49 Āsavasāsavadukādi, Sanidassanattika
Āsavasāsavadukādi, Sanidassanattika
97.
257Naāsavañceva naanāsavañca nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.
258Naanāsavañceva nano ca āsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
259(Naāsavañceva naanāsavaṃ naanidassanasappaṭighamūlepi purimanayena tīṇiyeva.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe ekaṃ…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
260Naāsavañceva naanāsavañca naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
261(Āsavaāsavasampayuttaduke pañhā na labbhati.)
