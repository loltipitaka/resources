2.10 Dasakapuggalapaññatti
Niddesa
Dasakapuggalapaññatti
209.
546Katamesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha niṭṭhā? Sattakkhattuparamassa, kolaṃkolassa, ekabījissa, sakadāgāmissa, yo ca diṭṭheva dhamme arahā—  imesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha niṭṭhā. (1--5)
547Katamesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha vihāya niṭṭhā? Antarāparinibbāyissa, upahaccaparinibbāyissa, asaṅkhāraparinibbāyissa, sasaṅkhāraparinibbāyissa, uddhaṃsotassa akaniṭṭhagāmino—  imesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha vihāya niṭṭhāti. (6--10)
548Ettāvatā puggalānaṃ puggalapaññattīti.
549Dasakaniddeso.
550Puggalapaññattipakaraṇaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
