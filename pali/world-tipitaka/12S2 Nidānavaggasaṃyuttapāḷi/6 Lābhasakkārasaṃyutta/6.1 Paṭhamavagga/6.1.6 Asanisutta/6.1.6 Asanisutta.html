6.1.6 Asanisutta
Lābhasakkārasaṃyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Asanisutta
162.
1033Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  adhigamāya. Kaṃ, bhikkhave, asanivicakkaṃ āgacchatu, sekhaṃ appattamānasaṃ lābhasakkārasiloko anupāpuṇātu.
1034Asanivicakkanti kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasilokassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.
1035Chaṭṭhaṃ.
