6.1.7 Diddhasutta
Lābhasakkārasaṃyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Diddhasutta
163.
1036Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  adhigamāya. Kaṃ, bhikkhave, diddhagatena visallena sallena vijjhatu, sekhaṃ appattamānasaṃ lābhasakkārasiloko anupāpuṇātu.
1037Sallanti kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasilokassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.
1038Sattamaṃ.
