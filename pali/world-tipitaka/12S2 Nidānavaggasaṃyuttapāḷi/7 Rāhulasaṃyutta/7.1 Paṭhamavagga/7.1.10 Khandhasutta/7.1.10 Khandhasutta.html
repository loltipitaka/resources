7.1.10 Khandhasutta
Rāhulasaṃyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Khandhasutta
197.
1132Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Taṃ kiṃ maññasi, rāhula, rūpaṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti? “Aniccaṃ, bhante”…  “vedanā…pe…  saññā…  saṅkhārā…  viññāṇaṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti? “Aniccaṃ, bhante”…  “evaṃ passaṃ, rāhula, sutavā ariyasāvako rūpasmimpi nibbindati…pe…  vedanāyapi nibbindati…  saññāyapi nibbindati…  saṅkhāresupi nibbindati…  viññāṇasmimpi nibbindati; nibbindaṃ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati; vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.
1133Dasamaṃ.
1134Paṭhamo vaggo.
1135Tassuddānaṃ
1136
Cakkhu rūpañca viññāṇaṃ,
samphasso vedanāya ca;
Saññā sañcetanā taṇhā,
dhātu khandhena te dasāti.
