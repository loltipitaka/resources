4.1.10 Puggalasutta
Anamataggasaṃyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Puggalasutta
133.
853Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “bhikkhavo”ti. “Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṃ. Bhagavā etadavoca— 
854“Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro…pe…  ekapuggalassa, bhikkhave, kappaṃ sandhāvato saṃsarato siyā evaṃ mahā aṭṭhikaṅkalo aṭṭhipuñjo aṭṭhirāsi yathāyaṃ vepullo pabbato, sace saṃhārako assa, sambhatañca na vinasseyya. Taṃ kissa hetu? Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.
855Idamavoca bhagavā. Idaṃ vatvāna sugato athāparaṃ etadavoca satthā— 
856
“Ekassekena kappena,
puggalassaṭṭhisañcayo;
Siyā pabbatasamo rāsi,
iti vuttaṃ mahesinā.
857
So kho panāyaṃ akkhāto,
vepullo pabbato mahā;
Uttaro gijjhakūṭassa,
magadhānaṃ giribbaje.
858
Yato ca ariyasaccāni,
sammappaññāya passati;
Dukkhaṃ dukkhasamuppādaṃ,
dukkhassa ca atikkamaṃ;
Ariyaṃ caṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ,
dukkhūpasamagāminaṃ.
859
Sa sattakkhattuṃparamaṃ,
sandhāvitvāna puggalo;
Dukkhassantakaro hoti,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayā”ti.
860Dasamaṃ.
861Paṭhamo vaggo.
862Tassuddānaṃ
863
Tiṇakaṭṭhañca pathavī,
assu khīrañca pabbataṃ;
Sāsapā sāvakā gaṅgā,
daṇḍo ca puggalena cāti.
