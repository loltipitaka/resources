1.1.2.9 Paṭhamadukkhuppādasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṃyutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Yamakavagga
Paṭhamadukkhuppādasutta
21.
54“Yo, bhikkhave, cakkhussa uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo sotassa…pe…  yo ghānassa…  yo jivhāya…  yo kāyassa…  yo manassa uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo.
55Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, cakkhussa nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo. Yo sotassa…  yo ghānassa…  yo jivhāya…  yo kāyassa…  yo manassa nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.
56Navamaṃ.
