1.3.5.9 Indriyasampannasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṃyutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Navapurāṇavagga
Indriyasampannasutta
154.
849Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami…pe…  ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “‘indriyasampanno, indriyasampanno’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, indriyasampanno hotī”ti?
850“Cakkhundriye ce, bhikkhu, udayabbayānupassī viharanto cakkhundriye nibbindati…pe…  jivhindriye ce, bhikkhu, udayabbayānupassī viharanto jivhindriye nibbindati…pe…  manindriye ce, bhikkhu, udayabbayānupassī viharanto manindriye nibbindati. Nibbindaṃ virajjati…pe…  vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānāti. Ettāvatā kho, bhikkhu, indriyasampanno hotī”ti.
851Navamaṃ.
