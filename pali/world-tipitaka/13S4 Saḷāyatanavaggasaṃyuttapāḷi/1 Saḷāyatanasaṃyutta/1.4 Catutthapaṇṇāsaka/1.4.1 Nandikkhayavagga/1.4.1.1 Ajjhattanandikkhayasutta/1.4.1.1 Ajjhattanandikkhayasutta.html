1.4.1.1 Ajjhattanandikkhayasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṃyutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Nandikkhayavagga
Ajjhattanandikkhayasutta
156.
861“Aniccaṃyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu cakkhuṃ aniccanti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṃ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo. Nandirāgakkhayā cittaṃ suvimuttanti vuccati…pe…  aniccaṃyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu jivhaṃ aniccanti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṃ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā…pe…  cittaṃ suvimuttanti vuccati…pe…  aniccaṃyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu manaṃ aniccanti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṃ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo. Nandirāgakkhayā cittaṃ suvimuttanti vuccatī”ti.
862Paṭhamaṃ.
