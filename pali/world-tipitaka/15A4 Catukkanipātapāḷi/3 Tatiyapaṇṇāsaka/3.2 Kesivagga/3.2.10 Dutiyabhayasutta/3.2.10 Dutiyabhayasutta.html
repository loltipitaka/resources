3.2.10 Dutiyabhayasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Kesivagga
Dutiyabhayasutta
120.
778“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni. Katamāni cattāri? Aggibhayaṃ, udakabhayaṃ, rājabhayaṃ, corabhayaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti.
779Dasamaṃ.
780Kesivaggo dutiyo.
781Tassuddānaṃ
782
Kesi javo patodo ca,
Nāgo ṭhānena pañcamaṃ;
Appamādo ca ārakkho,
Saṃvejanīyañca dve bhayāti.
