3.2.9 Paṭhamabhayasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Kesivagga
Paṭhamabhayasutta
119.
776“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni. Katamāni cattāri? Jātibhayaṃ, jarābhayaṃ, byādhibhayaṃ, maraṇabhayaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti.
777Navamaṃ.
