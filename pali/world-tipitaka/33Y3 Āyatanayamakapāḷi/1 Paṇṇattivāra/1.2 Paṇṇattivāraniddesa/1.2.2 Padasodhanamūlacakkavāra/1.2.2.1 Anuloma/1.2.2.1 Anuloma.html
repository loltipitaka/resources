247»  Cakkhu cakkhāyatananti?
248Dibbacakkhu paññācakkhu cakkhu, na cakkhāyatanaṃ. Cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhu ceva cakkhāyatanañca.
249«  Āyatanā sotāyatananti?
250Sotāyatanaṃ āyatanañceva sotāyatanañca. Avasesā āyatanā na sotāyatanaṃ.
251»  Cakkhu cakkhāyatananti?
252Dibbacakkhu paññācakkhu cakkhu, na cakkhāyatanaṃ. Cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhu ceva cakkhāyatanañca.

254Dhammāyatanaṃ āyatanañceva dhammāyatanañca. Avasesā āyatanā na dhammāyatanaṃ.

257Dhammāyatanaṃ ṭhapetvā avaseso dhammo, na dhammāyatanaṃ. Dhammāyatanaṃ dhammo ceva dhammāyatanañca.

259Cakkhāyatanaṃ āyatanañceva cakkhāyatanañca. Avasesā āyatanā na cakkhāyatanaṃ.
260»  Dhammo dhammāyatananti?
261Dhammāyatanaṃ ṭhapetvā avaseso dhammo, na dhammāyatanaṃ. Dhammāyatanaṃ dhammo ceva dhammāyatanañca.
