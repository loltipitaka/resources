2.3.5.3 Anulomapuggalokāsa
Pavattivāra
Uppādanirodhavāra
Paccuppannānāgatavāra
Anulomapuggalokāsa
235.
1808»  Yassa yattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1809«  Yassa vā pana yattha sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati tassa tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjatīti?
1810Pañcavokārā cavantānaṃ acakkhukānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati. Sacakkhukānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha sotāyatanañca nirujjhissati cakkhāyatanañca uppajjati.
1811»  Yassa yattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha ghānāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti?
1812Rūpāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati, no ca tesaṃ tattha ghānāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati. Sacakkhukānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanañca uppajjati ghānāyatanañca nirujjhissati.
1813«  Yassa vā pana yattha ghānāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati tassa tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjatīti?
1814Kāmāvacarā cavantānaṃ acakkhukānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha ghānāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati. Sacakkhukānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha ghānāyatanañca nirujjhissati cakkhāyatanañca uppajjati.
1815»  Yassa yattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha rūpāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1816«  Yassa vā pana yattha rūpāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati tassa tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjatīti?
1817Pañcavokārā cavantānaṃ acakkhukānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha rūpāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati. Sacakkhukānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha rūpāyatanañca nirujjhissati cakkhāyatanañca uppajjati.
1818»  Yassa yattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha manāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1819«  Yassa vā pana yattha manāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati tassa tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjatīti?
1820Pañcavokārā cavantānaṃ acakkhukānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ arūpānaṃ tesaṃ tattha manāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati. Sacakkhukānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha manāyatanañca nirujjhissati cakkhāyatanañca uppajjati.
1821»  Yassa yattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1822«  Yassa vā pana yattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati tassa tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjatīti?
1823Sabbesaṃ cavantānaṃ acakkhukānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ uppajjati. Sacakkhukānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dhammāyatanañca nirujjhissati cakkhāyatanañca uppajjati. (Cakkhāyatanamūlakaṃ.)
236.
1824»  Yassa yattha ghānāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha rūpāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1825«  Yassa vā pana yattha rūpāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati tassa tattha ghānāyatanaṃ uppajjatīti?
1826Kāmāvacarā cavantānaṃ aghānakānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ rūpāvacarānaṃ tesaṃ tattha rūpāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha ghānāyatanaṃ uppajjati. Saghānakānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha rūpāyatanañca nirujjhissati ghānāyatanañca uppajjati.
1827»  Yassa yattha ghānāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha manāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1828«  Yassa vā pana yattha manāyatanaṃ…pe… ?
1829Kāmāvacarā cavantānaṃ aghānakānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ rūpāvacarānaṃ arūpāvacarānaṃ tesaṃ tattha manāyatanaṃ…pe…  saghānakānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha manāyatanañca nirujjhissati ghānāyatanañca uppajjati.
1830»  Yassa yattha ghānāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1831«  Yassa vā pana yattha dhammāyatanaṃ…pe… ?
1832Sabbesaṃ cavantānaṃ aghānakānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dhammāyatanaṃ…pe…  saghānakānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dhammāyatanañca nirujjhissati ghānāyatanañca uppajjati. (Ghānāyatanamūlakaṃ.)
237.
1833»  Yassa yattha rūpāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha manāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti?
1834Asaññasattaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha rūpāyatanaṃ uppajjati, no ca tesaṃ tattha…pe…  pañcavokāraṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha…pe… .
1835«  Yassa vā pana yattha manāyatanaṃ…pe… ?
1836Pañcavokārā cavantānaṃ arūpānaṃ tesaṃ tattha…pe…  pañcavokāraṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha manāyatanañca nirujjhissati rūpāyatanañca uppajjati.
1837»  Yassa yattha rūpāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1838«  Yassa vā pana yattha dhammāyatanaṃ…pe… ?
1839Sabbesaṃ cavantānaṃ arūpakānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha rūpāyatanaṃ uppajjati. Sarūpakānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dhammāyatanañca nirujjhissati rūpāyatanañca uppajjati.
238.
1840»  Yassa yattha manāyatanaṃ uppajjati tassa tattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
1841«  Yassa vā pana yattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati tassa tattha manāyatanaṃ uppajjatīti?
1842Sabbesaṃ cavantānaṃ acittakānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dhammāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ tattha manāyatanaṃ uppajjati. Sacittakānaṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha dhammāyatanañca nirujjhissati manāyatanañca uppajjati.
