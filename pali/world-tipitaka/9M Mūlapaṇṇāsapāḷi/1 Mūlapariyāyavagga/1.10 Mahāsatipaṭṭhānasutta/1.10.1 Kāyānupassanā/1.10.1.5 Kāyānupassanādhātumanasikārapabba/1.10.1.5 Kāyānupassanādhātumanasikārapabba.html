1.10.1.5 Kāyānupassanādhātumanasikārapabba
Mūlapariyāyavagga
Mahāsatipaṭṭhānasutta
Kāyānupassanā
Kāyānupassanādhātumanasikārapabba
111.
321Puna caparaṃ, bhikkhave, bhikkhu imameva kāyaṃ yathāṭhitaṃ yathāpaṇihitaṃ dhātuso paccavekkhati—  ‘atthi imasmiṃ kāye pathavīdhātu āpodhātu tejodhātu vāyodhātū’ti.
322Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, dakkho goghātako vā goghātakantevāsī vā gāviṃ vadhitvā catumahāpathe bilaso vibhajitvā nisinno assa. Evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu imameva kāyaṃ yathāṭhitaṃ yathāpaṇihitaṃ dhātuso paccavekkhati—  ‘atthi imasmiṃ kāye pathavīdhātu āpodhātu tejodhātu vāyodhātū’ti.
323Iti ajjhattaṃ vā kāye kāyānupassī viharati…pe…  evampi kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati.
324Dhātumanasikārapabbaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
