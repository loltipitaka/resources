6.11 Pañcasataāgantukabhikkhuvatthu
Paṇḍitavagga
Pañcasataāgantukabhikkhuvatthu
87.
93
Kaṇhaṃ dhammaṃ vippahāya,
Sukkaṃ bhāvetha paṇḍito;
Okā anokamāgamma,
Viveke yattha dūramaṃ.
88.
94
Tatrābhiratimiccheyya,
hitvā kāme akiñcano;
Pariyodapeyya attānaṃ,
cittaklesehi paṇḍito.
89.
95
Yesaṃ sambodhiyaṅgesu,
sammā cittaṃ subhāvitaṃ;
Ādānapaṭinissagge,
anupādāya ye ratā;
Khīṇāsavā jutimanto,
te loke parinibbutā.
96Paṇḍitavaggo chaṭṭho.
