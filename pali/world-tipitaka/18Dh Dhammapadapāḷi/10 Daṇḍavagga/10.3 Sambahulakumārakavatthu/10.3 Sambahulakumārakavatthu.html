10.3 Sambahulakumārakavatthu
Daṇḍavagga
Sambahulakumārakavatthu
131.
141
Sukhakāmāni bhūtāni,
yo daṇḍena vihiṃsati;
Attano sukhamesāno,
pecca so na labhate sukhaṃ.
132.
142
Sukhakāmāni bhūtāni,
yo daṇḍena na hiṃsati;
Attano sukhamesāno,
pecca so labhate sukhaṃ.
