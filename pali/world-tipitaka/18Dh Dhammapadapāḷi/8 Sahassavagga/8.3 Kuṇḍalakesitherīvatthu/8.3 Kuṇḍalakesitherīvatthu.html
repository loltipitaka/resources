8.3 Kuṇḍalakesitherīvatthu
Sahassavagga
Kuṇḍalakesitherīvatthu
102.
110
Yo ca gāthā sataṃ bhāse,
anatthapadasaṃhitā;
Ekaṃ dhammapadaṃ seyyo,
yaṃ sutvā upasammati.
103.
111
Yo sahassaṃ sahassena,
saṅgāme mānuse jine;
Ekañca jeyyamattānaṃ,
sa ve saṅgāmajuttamo.
