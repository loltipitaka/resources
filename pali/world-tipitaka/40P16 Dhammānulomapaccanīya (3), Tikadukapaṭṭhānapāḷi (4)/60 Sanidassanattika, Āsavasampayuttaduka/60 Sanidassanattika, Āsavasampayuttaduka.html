Sanidassanattika, Āsavasampayuttaduka
103.
253Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto ca naanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
254Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
