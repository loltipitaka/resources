85 Sanidassanattika, Pītisahagataduka
Sanidassanattika, Pītisahagataduka
136.
326Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  anidassanaappaṭighaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho napītisahagato ca naanidassanasappaṭigho napītisahagato ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
327Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ napītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nanapītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
