48 Sanidassanattika, Hetusampayuttaduka
Sanidassanattika, Hetusampayuttaduka
91.
227Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  anidassanaappaṭighaṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetusampayutto ca naanidassanasappaṭigho nahetusampayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
228Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ hetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
