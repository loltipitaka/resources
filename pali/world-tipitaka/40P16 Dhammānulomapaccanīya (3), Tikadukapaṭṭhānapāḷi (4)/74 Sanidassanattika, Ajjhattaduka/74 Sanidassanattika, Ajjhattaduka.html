122.
293Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ ajjhattikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho naajjhattiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ ajjhattikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naajjhattiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  anidassanaappaṭighaṃ ajjhattikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naajjhattiko ca naanidassanasappaṭigho naajjhattiko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha. Ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
294Anidassanasappaṭighaṃ bāhiraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nabāhiro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekādasa, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate ekādasa.
