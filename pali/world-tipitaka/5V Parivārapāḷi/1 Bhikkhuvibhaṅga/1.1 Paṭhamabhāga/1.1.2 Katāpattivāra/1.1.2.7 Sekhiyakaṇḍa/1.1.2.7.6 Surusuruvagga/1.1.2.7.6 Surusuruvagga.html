1.1.2.7.6 Surusuruvagga
Bhikkhuvibhaṅga
Paṭhamabhāga
Katāpattivāra
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Surusuruvagga
180.
546Anādariyaṃ paṭicca surusurukārakaṃ bhuñjanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (1)
547Anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthanillehakaṃ bhuñjanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (2)
548Anādariyaṃ paṭicca pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (3)
549Anādariyaṃ paṭicca oṭṭhanillehakaṃ bhuñjanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (4)
550Anādariyaṃ paṭicca sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṃ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (5)
551Anādariyaṃ paṭicca sasitthakaṃ pattadhovanaṃ antaraghare chaḍḍento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (6)
552Anādariyaṃ paṭicca chattapāṇissa dhammaṃ desento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (7)
553Anādariyaṃ paṭicca daṇḍapāṇissa dhammaṃ desento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (8)
554Anādariyaṃ paṭicca satthapāṇissa dhammaṃ desento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (9)
555Anādariyaṃ paṭicca āvudhapāṇissa dhammaṃ desento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (10)
556Surusuruvaggo chaṭṭho.
