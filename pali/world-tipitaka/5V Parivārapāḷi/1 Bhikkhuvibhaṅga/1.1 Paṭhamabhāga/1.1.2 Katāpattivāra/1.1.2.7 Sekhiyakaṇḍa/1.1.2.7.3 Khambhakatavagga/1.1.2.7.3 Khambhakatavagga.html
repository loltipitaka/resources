1.1.2.7.3 Khambhakatavagga
Bhikkhuvibhaṅga
Paṭhamabhāga
Katāpattivāra
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
177.
513Anādariyaṃ paṭicca khambhakato antaraghare gacchanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (1)
514Anādariyaṃ paṭicca khambhakato antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (2)
515Anādariyaṃ paṭicca oguṇṭhito antaraghare gacchanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (3)
516Anādariyaṃ paṭicca oguṇṭhito antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (4)
517Anādariyaṃ paṭicca ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (5)
518Anādariyaṃ paṭicca pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (6)
519Anādariyaṃ paṭicca asakkaccaṃ piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (7)
520Anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (8)
521Anādariyaṃ paṭicca sūpaññeva bahuṃ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (9)
522Anādariyaṃ paṭicca thūpīkataṃ piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (10)
523Khambhakatavaggo tatiyo.
