1.1.2.7.1 Parimaṇḍalavagga
Bhikkhuvibhaṅga
Paṭhamabhāga
Katāpattivāra
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Parimaṇḍalavagga
175.
491Anādariyaṃ paṭicca purato vā pacchato vā olambento nivāsento kati āpattiyo āpajjati? Anādariyaṃ paṭicca purato vā pacchato vā olambento nivāsento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ—  anādariyaṃ paṭicca purato vā pacchato vā olambento nivāsento imaṃ ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. (1)
492Anādariyaṃ paṭicca purato vā pacchato vā olambento pārupanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (2)
493Anādariyaṃ paṭicca kāyaṃ vivaritvā antaraghare gacchanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (3)
494Anādariyaṃ paṭicca kāyaṃ vivaritvā antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (4)
495Anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthaṃ vā pādaṃ vā kīḷāpento antaraghare gacchanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (5)
496Anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthaṃ vā pādaṃ vā kīḷāpento antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (6)
497Anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento antaraghare gacchanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (7)
498Anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (8)
499Anādariyaṃ paṭicca ukkhittakāya antaraghare gacchanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (9)
500Anādariyaṃ paṭicca ukkhittakāya antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṃ. (10)
501Parimaṇḍalavaggo paṭhamo.
