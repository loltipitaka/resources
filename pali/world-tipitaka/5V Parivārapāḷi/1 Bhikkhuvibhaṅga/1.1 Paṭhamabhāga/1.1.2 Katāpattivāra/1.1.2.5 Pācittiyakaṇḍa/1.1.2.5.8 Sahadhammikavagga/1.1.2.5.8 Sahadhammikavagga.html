1.1.2.5.8 Sahadhammikavagga
Bhikkhuvibhaṅga
Paṭhamabhāga
Katāpattivāra
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Sahadhammikavagga
172.
462Bhikkhūhi sahadhammikaṃ vuccamāno—  “na tāvāhaṃ, āvuso, etasmiṃ sikkhāpade sikkhissāmi yāva na aññaṃ bhikkhuṃ byattaṃ vinayadharaṃ paripucchissāmī”ti bhaṇanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Bhaṇati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; bhaṇite āpatti pācittiyassa. (1)
463Vinayaṃ vivaṇṇento dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Vivaṇṇeti, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; vivaṇṇite āpatti pācittiyassa. (2)
464Mohento dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Anāropite mohe moheti, āpatti dukkaṭassa; āropite mohe moheti, āpatti pācittiyassa. (3)
465Bhikkhussa kupito anattamano pahāraṃ dento dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Paharati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; pahate āpatti pācittiyassa. (4)
466Bhikkhussa kupito anattamano talasattikaṃ uggiranto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Uggirati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; uggirite āpatti pācittiyassa. (5)
467Bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsento dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Anuddhaṃseti, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; anuddhaṃsite āpatti pācittiyassa. (6)
468Bhikkhussa sañcicca kukkuccaṃ upadahanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Upadahati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; upadahite āpatti pācittiyassa. (7)
469Bhikkhūnaṃ bhaṇḍanajātānaṃ kalahajātānaṃ vivādāpannānaṃ upassutiṃ tiṭṭhanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. “Sossāmī”ti gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa; yattha ṭhito suṇāti, āpatti pācittiyassa. (8)
470Dhammikānaṃ kammānaṃ chandaṃ datvā pacchā khīyanadhammaṃ āpajjanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Khiyyati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; khiyyite āpatti pācittiyassa. (9)
471Saṃghe vinicchayakathāya vattamānāya chandaṃ adatvā uṭṭhāyāsanā pakkamanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Parisāya hatthapāsaṃ vijahantassa āpatti dukkaṭassa; vijahite āpatti pācittiyassa. (10)
472Samaggena saṃghena cīvaraṃ datvā pacchā khīyanadhammaṃ āpajjanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Khiyyati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; khiyyite āpatti pācittiyassa. (11)
473Jānaṃ saṃghikaṃ lābhaṃ pariṇataṃ puggalassa pariṇāmento dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Pariṇāmeti, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; pariṇāmite āpatti pācittiyassa. (12)
474Sahadhammikavaggo aṭṭhamo.
