Vipattivāra
251.
1055Kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ vipattīnaṃ kati vipattiyo bhajanti? Kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ vipattīnaṃ dve vipattiyo bhajanti—  siyā sīlavipattiṃ, siyā ācāravipattiṃ…pe… . (1)
1056Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñjanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ vipattīnaṃ kati vipattiyo bhajanti? Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñjanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ vipattīnaṃ ekaṃ vipattiṃ bhajanti—  ācāravipattiṃ. (2)
1057Vipattivāro niṭṭhito tatiyo.
