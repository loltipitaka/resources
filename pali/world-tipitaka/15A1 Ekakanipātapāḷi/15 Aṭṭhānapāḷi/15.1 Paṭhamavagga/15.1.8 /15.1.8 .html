15.1.8
Aṭṭhānapāḷi
Paṭhamavagga
275.
365“Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ diṭṭhisampanno puggalo saṃghaṃ bhindeyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ puthujjano saṃghaṃ bhindeyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.
