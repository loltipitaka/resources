15.3.2--3
Aṭṭhānapāḷi
Tatiyavagga
288--289.
377“Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ vacīsucaritassa…pe…  manosucaritassa aniṭṭho akanto amanāpo vipāko nibbatteyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ manosucaritassa iṭṭho kanto manāpo vipāko nibbatteyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.
