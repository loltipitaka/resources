15.3.5--6
Aṭṭhānapāḷi
Tatiyavagga
291--292.
379“Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ vacīduccaritasamaṅgī…pe…  yaṃ manoduccaritasamaṅgī tannidānā tappaccayā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjeyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ manoduccaritasamaṅgī tannidānā tappaccayā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapajjeyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.
