10.1.1 Indakasutta
Yakkhasaṃyutta
Indakavagga
Indakasutta
235.
1451Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati indakūṭe pabbate, indakassa yakkhassa bhavane. Atha kho indako yakkho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi— 
1452
“Rūpaṃ na jīvanti vadanti buddhā,
Kathaṃ nvayaṃ vindatimaṃ sarīraṃ;
Kutassa aṭṭhīyakapiṇḍameti,
Kathaṃ nvayaṃ sajjati gabbharasmin”ti.
1453
“Paṭhamaṃ kalalaṃ hoti,
kalalā hoti abbudaṃ;
Abbudā jāyate pesi,
pesi nibbattatī ghano;
Ghanā pasākhā jāyanti,
kesā lomā nakhāpi ca.
1454
Yañcassa bhuñjatī mātā,
annaṃ pānañca bhojanaṃ;
Tena so tattha yāpeti,
mātukucchigato naro”ti.
