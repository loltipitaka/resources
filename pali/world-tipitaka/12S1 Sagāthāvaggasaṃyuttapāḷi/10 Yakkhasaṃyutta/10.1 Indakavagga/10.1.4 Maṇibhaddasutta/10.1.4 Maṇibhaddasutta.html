10.1.4 Maṇibhaddasutta
Yakkhasaṃyutta
Indakavagga
Maṇibhaddasutta
238.
1465Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā magadhesu viharati maṇimālike cetiye maṇibhaddassa yakkhassa bhavane. Atha kho maṇibhaddo yakkho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
1466
“Satīmato sadā bhaddaṃ,
satimā sukhamedhati;
Satīmato suve seyyo,
verā ca parimuccatī”ti.
1467
“Satīmato sadā bhaddaṃ,
satimā sukhamedhati;
Satīmato suve seyyo,
verā na parimuccati.
1468
Yassa sabbamahorattaṃ,
ahiṃsāya rato mano;
Mettaṃso sabbabhūtesu,
veraṃ tassa na kenacī”ti.
