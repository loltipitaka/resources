10.1.11 Cīrāsutta
Yakkhasaṃyutta
Indakavagga
Cīrāsutta
245.
1510Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Tena kho pana samayena aññataro upāsako cīrāya bhikkhuniyā cīvaraṃ adāsi. Atha kho cīrāya bhikkhuniyā abhippasanno yakkho rājagahe rathikāya rathikaṃ siṅghāṭakena siṅghāṭakaṃ upasaṅkamitvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
1511
“Puññaṃ vata pasavi bahuṃ,
Sappañño vatāyaṃ upāsako;
Yo cīrāya adāsi cīvaraṃ,
Sabbayogehi vippamuttiyā”ti.
