1.8.2 Rathasutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Chetvāvagga
Rathasutta
72.
337
“Kiṃsu rathassa paññāṇaṃ,
kiṃsu paññāṇamaggino;
Kiṃsu raṭṭhassa paññāṇaṃ,
kiṃsu paññāṇamitthiyā”ti.
338
“Dhajo rathassa paññāṇaṃ,
dhūmo paññāṇamaggino;
Rājā raṭṭhassa paññāṇaṃ,
bhattā paññāṇamitthiyā”ti.
