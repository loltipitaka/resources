1.6.3 Mittasutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Jarāvagga
Mittasutta
53.
292
“Kiṃsu pavasato mittaṃ,
kiṃsu mittaṃ sake ghare;
Kiṃ mittaṃ atthajātassa,
kiṃ mittaṃ samparāyikan”ti.
293
“Sattho pavasato mittaṃ,
mātā mittaṃ sake ghare;
Sahāyo atthajātassa,
hoti mittaṃ punappunaṃ;
Sayaṃkatāni puññāni,
taṃ mittaṃ samparāyikan”ti.
