1.4.7 Samayasutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Satullapakāyikavagga
Samayasutta
37.
205Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sakkesu viharati kapilavatthusmiṃ mahāvane mahatā bhikkhusaṃghena saddhiṃ pañcamattehi bhikkhusatehi sabbeheva arahantehi; dasahi ca lokadhātūhi devatā yebhuyyena sannipatitā honti bhagavantaṃ dassanāya bhikkhusaṃghañca. Atha kho catunnaṃ suddhāvāsakāyikānaṃ devatānaṃ etadahosi—  “ayaṃ kho bhagavā sakkesu viharati kapilavatthusmiṃ mahāvane mahatā bhikkhusaṃghena saddhiṃ pañcamattehi bhikkhusatehi sabbeheva arahantehi; dasahi ca lokadhātūhi devatā yebhuyyena sannipatitā honti bhagavantaṃ dassanāya bhikkhusaṃghañca. Yannūna mayampi yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkameyyāma; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavato santike paccekaṃ gāthaṃ bhāseyyāmā”ti.
206Atha kho tā devatā—  seyyathāpi nāma balavā puriso samiñjitaṃ vā bāhaṃ pasāreyya pasāritaṃ vā bāhaṃ samiñjeyya; evameva—  suddhāvāsesu devesu antarahitā bhagavato purato pāturahesuṃ. Atha kho tā devatā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhaṃsu. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho ekā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
207
“Mahāsamayo pavanasmiṃ,
Devakāyā samāgatā;
Āgatamha imaṃ dhammasamayaṃ,
Dakkhitāye aparājitasaṃghan”ti.
208Atha kho aparā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
209
“Tatra bhikkhavo samādahaṃsu,
Cittamattano ujukaṃ akaṃsu;
Sārathīva nettāni gahetvā,
Indriyāni rakkhanti paṇḍitā”ti.
210Atha kho aparā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
211
“Chetvā khīlaṃ chetvā palighaṃ,
Indakhīlaṃ ūhacca manejā;
Te caranti suddhā vimalā,
Cakkhumatā sudantā susunāgā”ti.
212Atha kho aparā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
213
“Ye keci buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gatāse,
Na te gamissanti apāyabhūmiṃ;
Pahāya mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
Devakāyaṃ paripūressantī”ti.
