1.5.1 Ādittasutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Ādittavagga
Ādittasutta
41.
238Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Atha kho aññatarā devatā abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇā kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi— 
239
“Ādittasmiṃ agārasmiṃ,
yaṃ nīharati bhājanaṃ;
Taṃ tassa hoti atthāya,
no ca yaṃ tattha ḍayhati.
240
Evaṃ ādittako loko,
jarāya maraṇena ca;
Nīharetheva dānena,
dinnaṃ hoti sunīhataṃ.
241
Dinnaṃ sukhaphalaṃ hoti,
nādinnaṃ hoti taṃ tathā;
Corā haranti rājāno,
aggi ḍahati nassati.
242
Atha antena jahati,
Sarīraṃ sapariggahaṃ;
Etadaññāya medhāvī,
Bhuñjetha ca dadetha ca;
Datvā ca bhutvā ca yathānubhāvaṃ,
Anindito saggamupeti ṭhānan”ti.
