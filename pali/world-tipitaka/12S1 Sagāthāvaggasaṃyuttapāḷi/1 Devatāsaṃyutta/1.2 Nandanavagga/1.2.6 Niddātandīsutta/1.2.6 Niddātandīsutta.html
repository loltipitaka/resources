1.2.6 Niddātandīsutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Nandanavagga
Niddātandīsutta
16.
53
“Niddā tandī vijambhitā,
Aratī bhattasammado;
Etena nappakāsati,
Ariyamaggo idha pāṇinan”ti.
54
“Niddaṃ tandiṃ vijambhitaṃ,
Aratiṃ bhattasammadaṃ;
Vīriyena naṃ paṇāmetvā,
Ariyamaggo visujjhatī”ti.
