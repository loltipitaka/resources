1.2.5 Saṇamānasutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Nandanavagga
Saṇamānasutta
15.
51
“Ṭhite majjhanhike kāle,
sannisīvesu pakkhisu;
Saṇateva brahāraññaṃ,
taṃ bhayaṃ paṭibhāti man”ti.
52
“Ṭhite majjhanhike kāle,
sannisīvesu pakkhisu;
Saṇateva brahāraññaṃ,
sā rati paṭibhāti man”ti.
