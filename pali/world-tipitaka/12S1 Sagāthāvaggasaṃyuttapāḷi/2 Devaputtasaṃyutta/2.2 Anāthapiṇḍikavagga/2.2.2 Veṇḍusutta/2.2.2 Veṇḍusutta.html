2.2.2 Veṇḍusutta
Devaputtasaṃyutta
Anāthapiṇḍikavagga
Veṇḍusutta
93.
433Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho veṇḍu devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
434
“Sukhitāva te manujā,
sugataṃ payirupāsiya;
Yuñjaṃ gotamasāsane,
appamattā nu sikkhare”ti.
435
“Ye me pavutte siṭṭhipade, (veṇḍūti bhagavā)
Anusikkhanti jhāyino;
Kāle te appamajjantā,
Na maccuvasagā siyun”ti.
