7.1.3 Asurindakasutta
Brāhmaṇasaṃyutta
Arahantavagga
Asurindakasutta
189.
1150Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Assosi kho asurindakabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo—  “bhāradvājagotto brāhmaṇo kira samaṇassa gotamassa santike agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajito”ti kupito anattamano yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ asabbhāhi pharusāhi vācāhi akkosati paribhāsati. Evaṃ vutte, bhagavā tuṇhī ahosi. Atha kho asurindakabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “jitosi, samaṇa, jitosi, samaṇā”ti.
1151
“Jayaṃ ve maññati bālo,
vācāya pharusaṃ bhaṇaṃ;
Jayañcevassa taṃ hoti,
yā titikkhā vijānato.
1152
Tasseva tena pāpiyo,
yo kuddhaṃ paṭikujjhati;
Kuddhaṃ appaṭikujjhanto,
saṅgāmaṃ jeti dujjayaṃ.
1153
Ubhinnamatthaṃ carati,
attano ca parassa ca;
Paraṃ saṅkupitaṃ ñatvā,
yo sato upasammati.
1154
Ubhinnaṃ tikicchantānaṃ,
attano ca parassa ca;
Janā maññanti bāloti,
ye dhammassa akovidā”ti.
1155Evaṃ vutte, asurindakabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “abhikkantaṃ, bho gotama…pe…  abbhaññāsi. Aññataro ca panāyasmā bhāradvājo arahataṃ ahosīti.
