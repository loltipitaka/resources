7.1.10 Bahudhītarasutta
Brāhmaṇasaṃyutta
Arahantavagga
Bahudhītarasutta
196.
1200Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe. Tena kho pana samayena aññatarassa bhāradvājagottassa brāhmaṇassa catuddasa balībaddā naṭṭhā honti. Atha kho bhāradvājagotto brāhmaṇo te balībadde gavesanto yena so vanasaṇḍo tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā addasa bhagavantaṃ tasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe nisinnaṃ pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya parimukhaṃ satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā. Disvāna yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi— 
1201
“Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,
Balībaddā catuddasa;
Ajjasaṭṭhiṃ na dissanti,
Tenāyaṃ samaṇo sukhī.
1202
Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,
Tilākhettasmi pāpakā;
Ekapaṇṇā dupaṇṇā ca,
Tenāyaṃ samaṇo sukhī.
1203
Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,
Tucchakoṭṭhasmi mūsikā;
Ussoḷhikāya naccanti,
Tenāyaṃ samaṇo sukhī.
1204
Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,
Santhāro sattamāsiko;
Uppāṭakehi sañchanno,
Tenāyaṃ samaṇo sukhī.
1205
Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,
Vidhavā satta dhītaro;
Ekaputtā duputtā ca,
Tenāyaṃ samaṇo sukhī.
1206
Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,
Piṅgalā tilakāhatā;
Sottaṃ pādena bodheti,
Tenāyaṃ samaṇo sukhī.
1207
Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,
Paccūsamhi iṇāyikā;
Detha dethāti codenti,
Tenāyaṃ samaṇo sukhī”ti.
1208
“Na hi mayhaṃ brāhmaṇa,
balībaddā catuddasa;
Ajjasaṭṭhiṃ na dissanti,
tenāhaṃ brāhmaṇā sukhī.
1209
Na hi mayhaṃ brāhmaṇa,
tilākhettasmi pāpakā;
Ekapaṇṇā dupaṇṇā ca,
tenāhaṃ brāhmaṇā sukhī.
1210
Na hi mayhaṃ brāhmaṇa,
tucchakoṭṭhasmi mūsikā;
Ussoḷhikāya naccanti,
tenāhaṃ brāhmaṇā sukhī.
1211
Na hi mayhaṃ brāhmaṇa,
santhāro sattamāsiko;
Uppāṭakehi sañchanno,
tenāhaṃ brāhmaṇā sukhī.
1212
Na hi mayhaṃ brāhmaṇa,
vidhavā satta dhītaro;
Ekaputtā duputtā ca,
tenāhaṃ brāhmaṇā sukhī.
1213
Na hi mayhaṃ brāhmaṇa,
piṅgalā tilakāhatā;
Sottaṃ pādena bodheti,
tenāhaṃ brāhmaṇā sukhī.
1214
Na hi mayhaṃ brāhmaṇa,
paccūsamhi iṇāyikā;
Detha dethāti codenti,
tenāhaṃ brāhmaṇā sukhī”ti.
1215Evaṃ vutte, bhāradvājagotto brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “abhikkantaṃ, bho gotama, abhikkantaṃ, bho gotama. Seyyathāpi, bho gotama, nikkujjitaṃ vā ukkujjeyya, paṭicchannaṃ vā vivareyya, mūḷhassa vā maggaṃ ācikkheyya, andhakāre vā telapajjotaṃ dhāreyya—  ‘cakkhumanto rūpāni dakkhantī’ti; evameva bhotā gotamena anekapariyāyena dhammo pakāsito. Esāhaṃ bhavantaṃ gotamaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi dhammañca bhikkhusaṃghañca. Labheyyāhaṃ bhoto gotamassa santike pabbajjaṃ, labheyyaṃ upasampadan”ti.
1216Alattha kho bhāradvājagotto brāhmaṇo bhagavato santike pabbajjaṃ, alattha upasampadaṃ. Acirūpasampanno panāyasmā bhāradvājo eko vūpakaṭṭho appamatto ātāpī pahitatto viharanto nacirasseva—  yassatthāya kulaputtā sammadeva agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajanti, tadanuttaraṃ—  brahmacariyapariyosānaṃ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja vihāsi. “Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā”ti abbhaññāsi. Aññataro ca panāyasmā bhāradvājo arahataṃ ahosīti.
1217Arahantavaggo paṭhamo.
1218Tassuddānaṃ
1219
Dhanañjānī ca akkosaṃ,
asurindaṃ bilaṅgikaṃ;
Ahiṃsakaṃ jaṭā ceva,
suddhikañceva aggikā;
Sundarikaṃ bahudhīta-
rena ca te dasāti.
