9.1.12 Majjhanhikasutta
Vanasaṃyutta
Vanavagga
Majjhanhikasutta
232.
1428Ekaṃ samayaṃ aññataro bhikkhu kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe. Atha kho tasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe adhivatthā devatā yena so bhikkhu tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā tassa bhikkhuno santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
1429
“Ṭhite majjhanhike kāle,
sannisīvesu pakkhisu;
Saṇateva brahāraññaṃ,
taṃ bhayaṃ paṭibhāti maṃ.
1430
Ṭhite majjhanhike kāle,
sannisīvesu pakkhisu;
Saṇateva brahāraññaṃ,
sā rati paṭibhāti man”ti.
