1.60 Gottenaanussāvanānujānana
Mahākhandhaka
Gottenaanussāvanānujānana
122.
511Tena kho pana samayena āyasmato mahākassapassa upasampadāpekkho hoti. Atha kho āyasmā mahākassapo āyasmato ānandassa santike dūtaṃ pāhesi—  “āgacchatu ānando imaṃ anussāvessatū”ti. Āyasmā ānando evamāha—  “nāhaṃ ussahāmi therassa nāmaṃ gahetuṃ, garu me thero”ti. Bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ. “Anujānāmi, bhikkhave, gottenapi anussāvetun”ti.
