7.7 Samādāyapannarasakādi
Kathinakkhandhaka
Samādāyapannarasakādi
316.
1446Bhikkhu atthatakathino cīvaraṃ samādāya pakkamati…pe… .
1447(Ādāyavārasadisaṃ evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
1448Bhikkhu atthatakathino vippakatacīvaraṃ ādāya pakkamati. Tassa bahisīmagatassa evaṃ hoti—  “idhevimaṃ cīvaraṃ kāressaṃ, na paccessan”ti. So taṃ cīvaraṃ kāreti. Tassa bhikkhuno niṭṭhānantiko kathinuddhāro…pe… .
1449(Samādāyavārasadisaṃ evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
