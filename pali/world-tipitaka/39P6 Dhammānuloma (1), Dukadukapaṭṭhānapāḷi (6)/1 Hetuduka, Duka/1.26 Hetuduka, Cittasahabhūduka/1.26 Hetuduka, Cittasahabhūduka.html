1.26 Hetuduka, Cittasahabhūduka
Hetuduka, Duka
Hetuduka, Cittasahabhūduka
50.
146Hetuṃ cittasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu cittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha nava.)
147Nahetuṃ nocittasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nocittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha ekaṃ. Sabbattha vitthāro.)
