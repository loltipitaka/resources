27Hetuṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu hetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu hetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu hetusampayutto ca nahetu hetusampayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
