1.28 Hetuduka, Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
Hetuduka, Duka
Hetuduka, Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
52.
150Hetuṃ cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha nava.)
151Nahetuṃ nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha ekaṃ. Sabbattha vitthāro.)
