1322Adhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya magga atthi avigatanti nahetuyā satta, naārammaṇe satta, naanantare satta, nasamanantare satta, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye satta, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte satta, naāsevane satta, nakamme satta, navipāke satta, naāhāre satta, najhāne satta, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.
