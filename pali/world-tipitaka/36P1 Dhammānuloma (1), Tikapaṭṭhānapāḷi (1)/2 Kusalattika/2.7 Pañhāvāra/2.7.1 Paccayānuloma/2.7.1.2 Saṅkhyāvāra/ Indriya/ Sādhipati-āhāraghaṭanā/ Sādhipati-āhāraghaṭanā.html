1095Indriyādhipati sahajāta nissaya āhāra atthi avigatanti satta. Indriyādhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya āhāra sampayutta atthi avigatanti tīṇi. Indriyādhipati sahajāta nissaya āhāra vippayutta atthi avigatanti tīṇi. [Avipāka—  3]
1096Indriyādhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka āhāra atthi avigatanti ekaṃ. Indriyādhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya vipāka āhāra sampayutta atthi avigatanti ekaṃ. Indriyādhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka āhāra vippayutta atthi avigatanti ekaṃ. [Savipāka—  3]
