Sādhipati-indriyaghaṭanā
370.
1123Maggādhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya atthi avigatanti satta. Maggādhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya indriya sampayutta atthi avigatanti tīṇi. Maggādhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya vippayutta atthi avigatanti tīṇi. [Avipāka—  3]
1124Maggādhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka indriya atthi avigatanti ekaṃ. Maggādhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya vipāka indriya sampayutta atthi avigatanti ekaṃ. Maggādhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka indriya vippayutta atthi avigatanti ekaṃ. [Savipāka—  3]
