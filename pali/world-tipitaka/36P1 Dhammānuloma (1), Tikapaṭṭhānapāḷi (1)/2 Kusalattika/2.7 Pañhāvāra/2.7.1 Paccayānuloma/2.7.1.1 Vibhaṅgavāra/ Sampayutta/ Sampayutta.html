Paccayānuloma
Vibhaṅgavāra
Sampayutta
297.
978Kusalo dhammo kusalassa dhammassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo—  kusalo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
979Akusalo dhammo akusalassa dhammassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo—  akusalo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
980Abyākato dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo—  vipākābyākato kiriyābyākato eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe vipākābyākato eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
