1739Napacchājātapaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasamanantarapaccayā nasahajātapaccayā naaññamaññapaccayā nanissayapaccayā upanissaye nava, kamme dve, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, atthiyā ekaṃ, avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
