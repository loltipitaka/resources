1720Nasahajātapaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasamanantarapaccayā naaññamaññapaccayā nanissayapaccayā upanissaye nava, pacchājāte tīṇi, kamme dve, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, vippayutte tīṇi, atthiyā pañca, avigate pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
