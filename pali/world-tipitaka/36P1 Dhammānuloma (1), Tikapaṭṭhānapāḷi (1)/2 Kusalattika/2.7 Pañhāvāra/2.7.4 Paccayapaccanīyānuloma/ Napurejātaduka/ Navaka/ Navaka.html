1735Napurejātapaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasamanantarapaccayā nasahajātapaccayā naaññamaññapaccayā upanissaye nava, pacchājāte tīṇi, kamme dve, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, vippayutte tīṇi, atthiyā pañca, avigate pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
