2.
124Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  kusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. (1)
125Akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  akusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. (1)
126Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  vipākābyākataṃ kiriyābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā; paṭisandhikkhaṇe vipākābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā, vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā. (1)
