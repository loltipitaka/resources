143Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo dhammo uppajjati purejātapaccayā—  kusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. Vatthuṃ purejātapaccayā. (1)
144Akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo dhammo uppajjati purejātapaccayā—  akusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. Vatthuṃ purejātapaccayā. (1)
145Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato dhammo uppajjati purejātapaccayā—  vipākābyākataṃ kiriyābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. Vatthuṃ purejātapaccayā. (1)
