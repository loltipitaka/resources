204.
698Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho kusalo dhammo uppajjati purejātapaccayā—  kusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe saṃsaṭṭho eko khandho, dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā, vatthuṃ purejātapaccayā. (1)
699Akusalaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho akusalo dhammo uppajjati purejātapaccayā—  akusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe saṃsaṭṭho eko khandho, dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā; vatthuṃ purejātapaccayā. (1)
700Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho abyākato dhammo uppajjati purejātapaccayā—  vipākābyākataṃ kiriyābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe saṃsaṭṭho eko khandho, dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā; vatthuṃ purejātapaccayā. (1)
