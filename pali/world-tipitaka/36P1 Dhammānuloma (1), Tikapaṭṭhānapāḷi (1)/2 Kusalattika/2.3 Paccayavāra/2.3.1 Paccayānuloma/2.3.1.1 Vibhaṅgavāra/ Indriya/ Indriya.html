482Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo dhammo uppajjati indriyapaccayā…pe…  asaññasattānaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paccayā…pe…  cakkhāyatanaṃ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṃ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ paccayā kāyaviññāṇaṃ, vatthuṃ paccayā vipākābyākatā kiriyābyākatā khandhā.
