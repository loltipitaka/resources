2590Upādinnupādāniyaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho upādinnupādāniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  upādinnupādāniyaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe saṃsaṭṭho eko khandho, dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā, paṭisandhikkhaṇe upādinnupādāniyaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā.
