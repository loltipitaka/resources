68.
2678Anupādinnupādāniyo dhammo anupādinnupādāniyassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā anupādinnupādāniyā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ anupādinnupādāniyānaṃ khandhānaṃ āsevanapaccayena paccayo. Anulomaṃ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṃ vodānassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2679Anupādinnupādāniyo dhammo anupādinnaanupādāniyassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṃ maggassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (2)
