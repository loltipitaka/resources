3.6 Sampayuttavāra
Vedanāttika
Sampayuttavāra
37.
1878Sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ sampayutto sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ sampayuttā dve khandhā, dve khandhe sampayutto eko khandho. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1879Sampayuttavāro.
