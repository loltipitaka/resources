Vibhaṅgavāra
Purejāta
9.
2642Paccuppanno dhammo paccuppannassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ—  cakkhuṃ…pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato…pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati, dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti, rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. Vatthupurejātaṃ—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa…  vatthu paccuppannānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
