99Hetuyā sattatiṃsa, ārammaṇe ekavīsa, adhipatiyā tevīsa, anantare ekavīsa, samanantare ekavīsa, sahajāte sattatiṃsa, aññamaññe aṭṭhavīsa, nissaye sattatiṃsa, upanissaye ekavīsa, purejāte ekādasa, āsevane ekādasa, kamme sattatiṃsa, vipāke sattatiṃsa, āhāre indriye jhāne magge sattatiṃsa, sampayutte ekavīsa, vippayutte sattatiṃsa, atthiyā sattatiṃsa, natthiyā ekavīsa, vigate ekavīsa, avigate sattatiṃsa.
