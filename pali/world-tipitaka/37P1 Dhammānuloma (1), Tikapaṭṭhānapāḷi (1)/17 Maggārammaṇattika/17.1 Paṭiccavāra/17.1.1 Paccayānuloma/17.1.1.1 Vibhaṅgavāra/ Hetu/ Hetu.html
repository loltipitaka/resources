2430Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca maggārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  maggārammaṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
2431Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca maggādhipati dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  maggārammaṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca maggādhipatī tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho. Dve khandhe…pe… . (2)
