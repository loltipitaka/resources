2595Uppanno dhammo uppannassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  uppannā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe uppannā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)
