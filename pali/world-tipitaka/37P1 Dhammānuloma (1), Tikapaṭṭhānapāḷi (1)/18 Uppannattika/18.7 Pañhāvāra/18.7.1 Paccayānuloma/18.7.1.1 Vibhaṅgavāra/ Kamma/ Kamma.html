2610Uppanno dhammo uppannassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  uppannā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe uppannā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
