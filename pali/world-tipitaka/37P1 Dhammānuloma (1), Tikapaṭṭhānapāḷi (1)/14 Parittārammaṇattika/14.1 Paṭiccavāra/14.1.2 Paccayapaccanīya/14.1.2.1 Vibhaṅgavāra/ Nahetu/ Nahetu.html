2163Parittārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukaṃ parittārammaṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhā.
