2157Parittārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  parittārammaṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhā. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe parittārammaṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhā. (1)
