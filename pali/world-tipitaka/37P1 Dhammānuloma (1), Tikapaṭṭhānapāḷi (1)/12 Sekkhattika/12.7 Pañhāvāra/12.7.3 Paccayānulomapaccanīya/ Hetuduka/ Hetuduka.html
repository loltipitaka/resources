Hetuduka
74.
1861Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe satta, naadhipatiyā satta, naanantare satta, nasamanantare satta, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye napurejāte napacchājāte naāsevane nakamme satta, navipāke cattāri, naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge satta, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
1862Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.
