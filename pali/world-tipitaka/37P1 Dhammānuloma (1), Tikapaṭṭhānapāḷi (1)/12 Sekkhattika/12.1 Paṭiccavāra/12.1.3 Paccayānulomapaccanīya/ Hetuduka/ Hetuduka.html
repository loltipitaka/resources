1699Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā tīṇi, naanantare nasamanantare naaññamaññe naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte naāsevane nava, nakamme dve, navipāke pañca, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
