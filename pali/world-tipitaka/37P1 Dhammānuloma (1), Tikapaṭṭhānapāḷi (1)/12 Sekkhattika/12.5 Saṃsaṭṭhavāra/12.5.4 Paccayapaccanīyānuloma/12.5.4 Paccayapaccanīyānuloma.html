12.5.4 Paccayapaccanīyānuloma
Sekkhattika
Saṃsaṭṭhavāra
Paccayapaccanīyānuloma
41.
1768Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe ekaṃ, anantare samanantare sahajāte aññamaññe nissaye upanissaye purejāte āsevane kamme vipāke āhāre indriye jhāne magge sampayutte vippayutte atthiyā natthiyā vigate avigate ekaṃ. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
1769Paccanīyānulomaṃ.
Saṃsaṭṭhavāro.
