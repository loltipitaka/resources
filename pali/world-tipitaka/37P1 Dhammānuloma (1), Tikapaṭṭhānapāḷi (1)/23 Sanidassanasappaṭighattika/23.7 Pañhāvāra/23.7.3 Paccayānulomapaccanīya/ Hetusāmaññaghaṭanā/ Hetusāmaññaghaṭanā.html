3096Hetu sahajāta nissaya atthi avigatanti naārammaṇe satta…pe…  naanantare satta, nasamanantare satta, naaññamaññe satta (idhāpi saṃkhittaṃ), nasampayutte satta, navippayutte ekaṃ, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.
3097Hetu sahajāta aññamañña nissaya atthi avigatanti naārammaṇe ekaṃ (sabbattha ekaṃ), novigate ekaṃ. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
