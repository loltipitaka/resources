45.
3034Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo—  anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ anidassanaappaṭighānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ anidassanaappaṭighānañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Kabaḷīkāro āhāro—  imassa anidassanaappaṭighassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3035Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo—  anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sanidassanasappaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sanidassanasappaṭighānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Kabaḷīkāro āhāro—  imassa sanidassanasappaṭighassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3036(Evaṃ satta pañhā pavattipaṭisandhi vibhajitabbā, sattasupi kabaḷīkāro āhāro kātabbo.) (7)
