1536Nahetuyā cattāri, naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā dvādasa, naanantare pañca, nasamanantare naaññamaññe naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte sattarasa, naāsevane ekādasa, nakamme satta, navipāke sattarasa, naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge ekaṃ, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
