Paccayānulomapaccanīya
Hetuduka
74.
1650Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe satta, naadhipatiyā naanantare nasamanantare satta, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye satta, napurejāte napacchājāte naāsevane nakamme navipāke naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge satta, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
1651Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.
