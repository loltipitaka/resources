Paccayapaccanīyānuloma
Nahetuduka
45.
2757Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā satta, anantare satta, samanantare satta, sahajāte tīṇi, aññamaññe tīṇi, nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye nava, āsevane tīṇi, kamme nava, vipāke tīṇi, āhāre indriye jhāne magge sampayutte atthiyā tīṇi, natthiyā satta, vigate satta, avigate tīṇi. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
2758Paccanīyānulomaṃ.
Pañhāvāro.
2759Atītārammaṇattikaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
