11.
1897Parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  paritte khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe paritte khandhe paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṃ, khandhe paṭicca vatthu…pe…  ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe… . (1)
1898Mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  mahaggate khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe mahaggate khandhe paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṃ. (1)
1899Appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  appamāṇe khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (1)
1900Parittañca appamāṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  appamāṇe khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (1)
1901Parittañca mahaggatañca dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  mahaggate khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe mahaggate khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṃ. (1)
