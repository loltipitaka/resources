Kamma
51.
2384Micchattaniyato dhammo micchattaniyatassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  micchattaniyatā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2385Micchattaniyato dhammo aniyatassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. Sahajātā—  micchattaniyatā cetanā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. Nānākkhaṇikā—  micchattaniyatā cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
2386Micchattaniyato dhammo micchattaniyatassa ca aniyatassa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  micchattaniyatā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
