Kusalattika, Atītattikadvaya
21.
71Nakusalo napaccuppanno dhammo kusalassa paccuppannassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
72Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā anantare samanantare soḷasa, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, natthiyā vigate soḷasa.
22.
73Nakusalaṃ naatītārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato atītārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
74Nakusalaṃ naanāgatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo anāgatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.
75Nakusalaṃ napaccuppannārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato paccuppannārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
