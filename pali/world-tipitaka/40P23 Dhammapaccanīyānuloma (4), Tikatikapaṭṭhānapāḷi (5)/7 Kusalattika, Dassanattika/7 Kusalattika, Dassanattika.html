11.
42Nakusalaṃ nadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo dassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
43Nakusalaṃ nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
44Nakusalaṃ nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
