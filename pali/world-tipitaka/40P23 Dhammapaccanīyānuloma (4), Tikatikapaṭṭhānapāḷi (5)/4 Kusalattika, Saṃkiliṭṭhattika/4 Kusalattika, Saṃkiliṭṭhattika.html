Kusalattika, Saṃkiliṭṭhattika

33Nakusalaṃ nasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . (Akusalāneva tīṇi. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
34Nakusalaṃ naasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.
35Nakusalaṃ naasaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe cha…pe…  āsevane tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
