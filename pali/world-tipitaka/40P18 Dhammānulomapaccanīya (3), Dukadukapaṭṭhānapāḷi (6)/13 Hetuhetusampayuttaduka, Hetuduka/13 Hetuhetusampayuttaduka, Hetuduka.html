13 Hetuhetusampayuttaduka, Hetuduka
Hetuhetusampayuttaduka, Hetuduka
46.
104Hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
105Hetusampayuttañceva na ca hetuṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetuvippayutto ceva nana ca hetu nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
