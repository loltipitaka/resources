Saraṇaduka, Hetudukādi
84.
194Saraṇaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
195Araṇaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cattāri.
196Saraṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
197Araṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
85.
198Saraṇaṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
199Araṇaṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā dve…pe…  sahajāte cattāri, aññamaññe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cattāri.
200Saraṇaṃ ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
201Araṇaṃ ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
86.
202Saraṇaṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. Araṇaṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
203Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā dve…pe…  sahajāte cattāri, aññamaññe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cattāri. (Sahetukadukasadisaṃ.)
87.
204Saraṇaṃ hetuñceva sahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nahetu ceva naahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
205Araṇaṃ hetuñceva sahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu ceva naahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
206Saraṇaṃ sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
207Araṇaṃ sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
