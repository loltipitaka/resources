17 Āsavasampayuttaduka, Hetuduka
Āsavasampayuttaduka, Hetuduka
55.
130Āsavasampayuttaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
131Āsavavippayuttaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavavippayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
132Āsavasampayuttaṃ hetuñca āsavavippayuttaṃ hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.
133Āsavasampayuttaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
134Āsavavippayuttaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cattāri…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cattāri.
