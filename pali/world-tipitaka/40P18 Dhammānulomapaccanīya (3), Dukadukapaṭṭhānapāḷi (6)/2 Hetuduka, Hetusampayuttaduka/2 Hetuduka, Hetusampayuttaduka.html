Hetuduka, Hetusampayuttaduka
5.
16Hetuṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
17Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe pañca, vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sahetukasadisaṃ.)
18Hetuṃ hetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nahetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
19Nahetuṃ hetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu nahetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
20Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe cattāri…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cattāri.
