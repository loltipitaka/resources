38.
86Hetuṃ dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
