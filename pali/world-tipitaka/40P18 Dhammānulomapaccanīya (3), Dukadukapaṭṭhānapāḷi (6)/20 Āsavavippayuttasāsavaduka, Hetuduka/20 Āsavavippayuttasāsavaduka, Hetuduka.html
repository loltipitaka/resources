20 Āsavavippayuttasāsavaduka, Hetuduka
Āsavavippayuttasāsavaduka, Hetuduka
58.
143Āsavavippayuttaṃ sāsavaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto naanāsavo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
144Āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto naanāsavo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  avigate cattāri.
145Āsavavippayuttaṃ sāsavaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto naanāsavo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto nasāsavo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.
